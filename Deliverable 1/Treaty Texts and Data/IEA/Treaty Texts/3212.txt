AMENDMENTS TO THE SCHEDULE
As a consequence of the decisions taken at the 47th Annual Meeting the following amendments to the Schedule were necessary:
Revise paragraphs 11 and 12, and Tables 1, 2 and 3 by substituting the dates 1995/1996 pelagic season, 1996 coastal season, 1996 season, or 1996 as appropriate.
