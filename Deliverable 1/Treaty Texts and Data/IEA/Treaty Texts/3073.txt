    ﻿Amendments to the Schedule to the International Convention for the Regulation of Whaling of 2 December 1946
Source: http://bar.austlii.edu.au/au/other/dfat/treaties/1990/54.html
The amendments read as follows (changes in bold type):
1. Revise paragraphs 11 and 12, and Tables 1, 2 and 3, by substitution of the dates 1990/91 pelagic season, 1991 coastal season, 1991 season, or 1991 as appropriate.
2. Amend paragraph 13(b)(4) by substitution of the dates 1990/91 and 1992/93.
