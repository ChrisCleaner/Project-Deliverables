    ﻿Amendments To The Schedule To The International Convention For The Regulation Of Whaling Of 2 December 1946
Source: AUSTRALIAN TREATY SERIES 1969 No. 38 (electronic)
The amendments are as follows:
Paragraph 4(1)(a)
Delete the words after "Ocean" and substitute the following: "for the three years ending on 24 February 1973".
Paragraph 6(1), line 2
Delete "1969" and substitute "1972".
Paragraph 8(a), line 3
Delete "3,200" and "1968/69" and substitute "2,700" and "1969/70" respectively.
