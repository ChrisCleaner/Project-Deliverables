    ﻿Amendment to Article VI(2) of the Agreement for the Establishment of the Indo-Pacific Fisheries Council of 26 February 1948
Source: http://www.austlii.edu.au/au/other/dfat/treaties/19520022.html
Replace the word "and" between the words "Chairman" and "Vice-Chairman" by a comma, and insert after the word "Vice-Chairman" the words "and immediately retired Chairman,".
Adopted by the Indo-Pacific Fisheries Council at its fourth meeting (Quezon City, 7 November 1952). The Amendment entered into force 9 December 1952.
