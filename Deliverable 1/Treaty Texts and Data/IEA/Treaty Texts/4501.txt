    ﻿Protocol extending the period of Signature of the Treaty concerning the Archipelago of Spitsbergen
Source: http://www.austlii.edu.au/au/other/dfat/treaties/1925/10.html
(Translation)
The plenipotentiaries who, in consequence of their absence from Paris for the time being, have been unable to affix their signature to the Treaty concerning Spitsbergen, signed this day, will be permitted to do so until 8 April 1920.
DONE at Paris, 9 February 1920.
[Signatures not reproduced here.]
