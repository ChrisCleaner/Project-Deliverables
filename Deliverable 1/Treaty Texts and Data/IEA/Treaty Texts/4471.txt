    ﻿Amendment Of The Preamble To The Convention Relating To The Status Of The River Gambia
Source: http://www.fao.org/docrep/W7414B/w7414b0b.htm, http://faolex.fao.org/docs/texts/gam15878.doc
The Preamble to this Convention was amended by Resolution 3/CEG/CG of 6 June 1981 of the Conference of Heads of State and Government [to reflect the adhesion of The People's Revolutionary Republic of Guinea to the Gambia River Basin Development Organization]. 
Original text: 
We, The Heads of State and Government of
The Republic of Senegal,
The Republic of the Gambia,
Amended text:
The Heads of State and Government of
The Republic of the Gambia,
The People's Revolutionary Republic of Guinea,
The Republic of Senegal,
