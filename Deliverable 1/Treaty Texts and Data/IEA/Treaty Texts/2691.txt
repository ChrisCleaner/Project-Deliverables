    ﻿Amendments of the Convention For The Regulation Of The Meshes Of Fishing Nets And The Size Limits Of Fish
Source:
The following Amendments were agreed at the Eighth Meeting of the Permanent Commission held in London from 3 to 6 May 1960:
(1) In article 6 of the Convention the words "Norway pout (Gadus esmarkii)"* should be inserted after the words "sand eels (Ammodytes)".
(2) In article 7 (2) of the Convention replace the words "5th April, 1961" in subparagraph 1 by the words "5th April, 1962".
(3) In Annex I (1) to the Convention replace the words "4th day of April, 1961"2 by the words "4th day of April, 1963".
