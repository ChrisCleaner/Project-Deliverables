    ﻿Amendment to Annex IV (II) 1 of the Agreement on the International Dolphin Conservation Program
Source: http://www.iattc.org/PDFFiles2/AIDCP-amendments-Oct-2009.pdf, downloaded 20120420
The following amendment was adopted on 5 June 2009 at the 21st meeting of the Parties.
Annex IV (II) 1:
NEW: Sentence added at end of paragraph:
Any vessel which is the subject of a pending request for exemption may fish until such time as the Parties decide the request, provided that such request was submitted in a timely manner.
