    ﻿Amendments to Annex IV(III)2, Annex IV(III)3, and Annex VIII(2)b of the Agreement on the International Dolphin Conservation Program
The following amendments were adopted on 30 October 2000 at the 4th meeting of the Parties.
Annex IV(III)2:
Original language:
On the first working day of April of each year, ….
Amended language:
No later than April 15 of each year, ….
Annex IV(III)3:
Original language:
… no later than May 1, …
Amended language:
… no later than May 5, …
Annex VIII(2)b:
Original language:
Have at least three operable speedboats. All operable speedboats shall be equipped with towing bridles or posts, and tow lines;
Amended language:
Have at least three operable speedboats equipped withoperable towing bridles or posts, and tow lines;
