Paragraph 1(a), line 3
Delete the words "the Antarctic".
Paragraph 1(b), line 1
Add after "station" the following sentence:
"There shall be maintained such observers as the member countries having jurisdiction over land stations may arrange to place at each other's land stations."
Paragraph 8(a), line 3
Delete "1967/68" and substitute "1968/69".
