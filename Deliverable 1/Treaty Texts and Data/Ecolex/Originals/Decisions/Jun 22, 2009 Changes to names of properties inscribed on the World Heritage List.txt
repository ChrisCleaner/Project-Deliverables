The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document  WHC-09/33.COM/8B , \n 2.  Approves  the proposed name change to Historical Monuments of Thatta as proposed by the Pakistani authorities. The name of the property becomes  Historical   Monuments   at Makli, Thatta  in English and  Monuments historiques &agrave; Makli, Thatta  in French.
