N&deg;:  883  \n State Party:  Bolivia    \n Criteria:  C(ii)(iii)  \n The Committee inscribed this site on the World Heritage List on the basis of  criteria  ( ii) and (iii) : \n  Criterion (ii) : The sculptured rock at Samaipata is the dominant ceremonial feature of an urban settlement that represents the apogee of this form of prehispanic religious and political centre. \n  Criterion (iii) : Samaipata bears outstanding witness to the existence in this Andean region of a culture with highly developed religious traditions, illustrated dramatically in the form of immense rock sculptures. \n &nbsp;
