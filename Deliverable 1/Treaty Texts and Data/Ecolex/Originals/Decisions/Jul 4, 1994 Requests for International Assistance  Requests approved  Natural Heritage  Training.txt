Training in the Conservation and Management of Natural Heritage in the Arab Region    \n The Bureau  reviewed  a request for US$30,000 for a two-week regional training seminar submitted by the Egyptian authorities, to be held in Egypt in April 1995 on conservation and management of natural heritage. \n The Bureau  approved  the requested sum of  US$30,000  for the course and asked the Centre to proceed with an agreement on the course programme with the national authorities.
