The World Heritage Committee,  1.  Adopts  the following Strategic Objectives:   Strengthen the Credibility of the World Heritage List;  Ensure the effective Conservation of World Heritage properties;  Promote the development of effective Capacity Building in States Parties;   Increase public awareness, involvement and support for World Heritage through Communication.  2. \xc2\xa0 Invites  the Director-General to develop performance indicators for each of the Strategic Objectives to be presented to the 27th session of the Committee (June/July 2003) to enable the Committee to measure the success of the implementation of the Objectives over time.
