The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having Examined  Document  WHC-09/33.COM/8A , \n 2.  Recalling  Decisions  31 COM 8.1  and  32 COM 8A  adopted at its 31st (Christchurch, 2007); and 32nd (Quebec City, 2008) sessions respectively, \n 3.  Stressing  the importance of the process of revision and updating of Tentative Lists, as a tool for the regional harmonization of the World Heritage List and of long term planning of its development; \n 4.  Takes note  of the Tentative Lists presented in Annexes 2 and 3 of this document; \n 5.  Also takes note  that several States Parties (including, among others, Afghanistan, Brazil, Israel, Libya, Oman, Paraguay, S&atilde;o Tom&eacute; e Principe and Uruguay) are currently preparing revised or updated Tentative Lists; \n 6.  Decides  to adjourn the discussion on these matters to its 34th session in 2010.
