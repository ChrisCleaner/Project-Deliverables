The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Having examined  Document WHC-12/36.COM/7B, \n 2.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Recalling  Decision  34 COM 7B.71 , adopted at its 34th session (Brasilia, 2010), \n 3.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Takes note  of the further reduction in height of the Jahan-Nama Building and  reiterates its request  to the State Party to confirm as soon as possible, in writing, to the World Heritage Centre, that the demolition has been completed; \n 4.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Requests  the State Party to submit, as a matter of urgency, to the World Heritage Centre, for review by the Advisory Bodies, detailed information and documentation on the proposed route of metro line&nbsp;2; \n 5.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Also reiterates its request  to the State Party to develop a Management Plan for the property, in consultation with all stakeholders, and to ensure that this Management Plan becomes part of a larger strategic vision for integrated urban development and conservation; \n 6.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Also requests  the State Party to invite a joint World Heritage Centre/ICOMOS reactive monitoring mission to the property to consider the impact of the current metro constructions on the property and its wider setting; \n 7.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Further requests  the State Party to submit maps precisely indicating the property boundaries and buffer zone; \n 8.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Requests furthermore  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 February 2013 , an updated report on the state of conservation of the property and the implementation of the above, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 37th session in 2013.
