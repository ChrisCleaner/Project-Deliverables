The General Assembly,      Having examined  Documents WHC-13/19.GA/7 and WHC-13/19.GA/INF.7,    Having in particular examined  the accounts of the World Heritage Fund for the financial period ended 31 December 2011 approved by the Senior Accountant of the UNESCO Bureau of Financial Management,    Takes note  of the accounts of the World Heritage Fund for 2012-2013, established by the UNESCO Bureau of Financial Management;    Also takes note  of the Chart representing the reductions of activities as requested in Decision  37 COM 15.I  taken by the World Heritage Committee at its 37th session;    Approves  the accounts of the World Heritage Fund for the financial period ended 31 December 2011.
