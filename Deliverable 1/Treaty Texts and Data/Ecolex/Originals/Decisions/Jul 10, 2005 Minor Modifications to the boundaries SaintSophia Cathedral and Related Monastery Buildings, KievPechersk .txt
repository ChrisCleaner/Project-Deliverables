The World Heritage Committee,  1.  Having examined  Document   WHC-05/29.COM/8B.Add  ;  2.  Commends  the State Party of Ukraine for having proposed the buffer zones;  3.  Approves  the minor modification of the buffer zone of Kiev:  Saint-Sophia   Cathedral and Related Monastery Buildings, Kiev-Pechersk  (Ukraine);  4.  Welcomes  the proposal by the State Party to organise a mission to the property that would as well examine the choice of the eastern boundary of the buffer zone and the existing quality and condition of the urban fabric of the central Kiev both inside and outside the buffer zone.
