The World Heritage Committee, \n \n  Having examined  Document WHC-14/38.COM/7B.Add, \n  Takes note with satisfaction of the measures taken by the States Parties concerned to address its previous requests to mitigate the threats on the Outstanding Universal Value of the following World Heritage properties:   \n \n \n  Town of Luang Prabang (Lao People\'s Democratic Republic),  \n  Historic Centre of Saint Petersburg and Related Groups of Monuments (Russian Federation),   \n  Historic Bridgetown and its Garrison (Barbados),   \n  Port, Fortresses and Group of Monuments, Cartagena (Colombia),   \n  Pre-Hispanic City of Teotihuacan (Mexico),   \n  Historic Quarter of the City of Colonia del Sacramento (Uruguay),  \n  Royal Palaces of Abomey (Benin),  \n  Rock-Hewn Churches, Lalibela (Ethiopia),   \n  Aapravasi Ghat (Mauritius);  \n \n \n  Encourages  the States Parties concerned to pursue their efforts to ensure the conservation of World Heritage properties; \n \xc2\xa0 Reminds  the States Parties concerned to inform the World Heritage Centre in due course about any major development project that may negatively impact the Outstanding Universal Value of a property, before any irreversible decisions are made, in line with Paragraph 172 of the  Operational Guidelines .
