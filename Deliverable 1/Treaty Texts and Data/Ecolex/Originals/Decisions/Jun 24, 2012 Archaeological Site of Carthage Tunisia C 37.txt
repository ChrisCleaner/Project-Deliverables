The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Having examined  Document WHC-12/36.COM/7B, \n 2.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Recalling  Decision  35   COM 7B.59 , adopted at its 35th session (UNESCO, 2011), \n 3.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Notes  the report presented by the State Party and the information provided on the property&rsquo;s boundaries; \n 4.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Encourages  the State Party to continue its policy of land management of the areas in the archaeological zone to avoid alteration to the integrity of the property; \n 5.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Requests  the State Party to implement the recommendations of the joint World Heritage Centre/ICOMOS reactive monitoring mission of January 2012, including: \n a)&nbsp; the revision, adoption and implementation of the Protection and Enhancement Plan for the property, \n b)&nbsp; the elaboration of a Presentation Plan and a Tourism Management Plan, \n c)&nbsp; the development of an archaeological and conservation strategy, \n d)&nbsp; the coordination of preservation and management tools of the property, and coordination of the roles of the different stakeholders concerned; \n 6.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Also requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1   February 2014  a report on progress made in implementing the above recommendations for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 38th session in 2014.&nbsp;
