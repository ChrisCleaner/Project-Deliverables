The  Ninth Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. IX/15:     Noting  the progress in the preparation of the guidelines for funding the production sector indicated in the report of the Executive Committee to the Ninth Meeting of the Parties,    Recognizing  the importance of timely phase-out of ozone-depleting substances in the countries operating under Article 5,    Recognizing  the equal importance of funding both the closure of facilities and the production of substitutes for ozone-depleting substances,    Recognizing  the importance of technology transfer for the effective implementation of the activities in the production sector,   To request the Executive Committee to accelerate the formulation of the guidelines for funding the production sector and the subsequent approval of relevant projects in this sector.
