The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document  WHC-09/33.COM/7A , \n 2.  Recalling  Decision  32 COM 7A.21 , adopted at its 32nd session (Quebec City, 2008), \n 3.  Takes note  of the report provided by the State Party on the state of conservation of the property, as requested by the World Heritage Committee at its 32nd session; \n 4.  Notes  the efforts and commitment of the State Party and the international community for the safeguarding of this property and  urges  the State Party to continue its work on the corrective measures, particularly the completion of the management plan for the property; \n 5.  Thanks  the governments of Germany and Japan for their support and  calls upon  the international community to continue providing technical and financial support, in particular to achieve the desired state of conservation; \n 6.  Reiterates its request  to the State Party to develop, in consultation with the World Heritage Centre and Advisory Bodies, a draft Statement of Outstanding Universal Value, for examination by the World Heritage Committee; \n 7.  Requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre by  1 February 2010  a progress report on the implementation of the corrective measures, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 34th session in 2010; \n 8.   Decides  to retain the Cultural Landscape and Archaeological Remains of the Bamiyan Valley (Afghanistan) on the List of World Heritage in Danger.
