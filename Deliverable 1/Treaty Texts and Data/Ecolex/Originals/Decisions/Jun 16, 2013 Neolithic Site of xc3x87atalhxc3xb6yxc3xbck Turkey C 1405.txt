The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;   Having examined   Document WHC-13/37.COM/7B, \n 2.&nbsp;   Recalling   Decision   36 COM 8B.36   , adopted at its 36th&nbsp;session (Saint-Petersburg, 2012), \n 3.&nbsp;   Welcomes   the progress made by the State Party with regards to the Management Plan and   takes note   of the information regarding its approval and imminent implementation; \n 4.&nbsp;   Also takes note   that the State Party intends to revise this Management Plan and   requests   it to ensure, within the framework of the revision process that: \n a)&nbsp; the actions listed in the Management Plan, including suitable monitoring indicators, are elaborated in a such a way that they enable the State Party to adequately monitor the conservation and management of the property, \n b)&nbsp; the legal underpinning of the Plan is secured, and that a more detailed financial strategy is elaborated to ensure that adequate funding is in place for all necessary actions; \n 5.&nbsp;   Also requests   the State Party to provide printed and electronic copies of the revised Management Plan to the World Heritage Centre by   1&nbsp;February 2014,   for review by the Advisory Bodies.
