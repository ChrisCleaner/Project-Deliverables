The World Heritage Committee,  1.  Accepts  as &quot;complete&quot; all nominations listed as &quot;complete&quot; and &quot;almost complete&quot; in Table A of document WHC-02/CONF.202/10 Rev and agrees that they be examined at the 27th session of the Committee in 2003;  2.  Accepts  for examination at the 27th session of the Committee in June/July 2003, on an exceptional basis in view of the transitional period, the three nominations: Rock Shelters of Bhimbetka (India), Takht-e Soleyman (Iran), and Matobo Hills (Zimbabwe), which have been made complete following the deadline of 1\xc2\xa0February 2002. (Consequently, the nomination of Rock Shelters of Bhimbetka, India, a site belonging to an underrepresented category, is by this action substituted for the nomination of Champaner-Pavagadh Archaeological Park, India, in Table A). In total, 28 nominations will be examined in 2003;  3.  Decides  that, in the future, all nominations will be accepted only if they are &quot;complete&quot; - as defined in the  Operational Guidelines  - at the time of the deadline and abolishes hereafter the category of &quot;almost complete&quot;; 4.  Requests  the Secretariat to establish and submit at each Committee session a list of all nominations received, including the date of reception, an indication of their status &quot;complete&quot; or &quot;incomplete&quot;, as well as the date at which they are considered as &quot;complete&quot; in conformity with the  Operational Guidelines .
