The World Heritage Committee, \n  Having examined  document WHC-12/36.COM/5B, \n  Recalling  Decision  35 COM 5B  adopted at its 35th session (UNESCO, 2011), \n  Takes note  of the reports of the Advisory Bodies (ICCROM, ICOMOS and IUCN) on their activities and  expresses its gratitude  for the efforts undertaken; \n  Welcomes  the harmonization of the reports by the Advisory Bodies and the comments on the progress made and gaps identified for the implementation of the  Convention .
