The  Eleventh Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. XI/20:      Recalling  decisions IX/24 and X/8 on control of new ozone-depleting substances,     Noting  that the issue was discussed at the Eleventh Meeting of the Parties,    To continue to give full consideration to ways to expedite the procedure for adding new substances and their associated control measures to the Protocol and for removing them therefrom.
