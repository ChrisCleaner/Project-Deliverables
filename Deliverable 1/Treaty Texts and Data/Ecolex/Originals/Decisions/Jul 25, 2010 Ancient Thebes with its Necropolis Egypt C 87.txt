The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document WHC-10/34.COM/7B, \n 2.  Recalling  Decision  33 COM 7B.54 , adopted at its 33rd session (Seville, 2009), \n 3.  Takes note  of the detailed state of conservation report provided by the State Party; \n 4.  Notes  that the report however does not respond to some of the requests made by the World Heritage Committee in previous decisions and  reiterates its request  for: \n a) an integrated management plan for the property as a whole, \n b) the establishment of a West Bank buffer zone, \n c) a draft Statement of Outstanding Universal Value; \n 5.  Requests  the State Party to provide detailed information on the planning and design of proposed and on-going projects, in particular for the Avenue of the Sphinx, the Corniche and the landing stage for cruise boats on the West Bank in line with the  Operational Guidelines ; \n 6.  Also requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 February 2012 , a detailed progress report on the implementation of the above, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 36th session in 2012.
