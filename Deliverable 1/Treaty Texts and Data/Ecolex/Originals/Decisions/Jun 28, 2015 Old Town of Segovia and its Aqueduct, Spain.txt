The World Heritage Committee, \n \n  Having examined  Documents WHC-15/39.COM/8B.Add and WHC-15/39.COM/INF.8B1.Add, \n  Approves  the proposed buffer zone for the  Old Town of Segovia and its Aqueduct, Spain ; \n  Requests   the State Party to submit  to the World Heritage Centre ,  by  1 December 2015 , a report outlining in detail how the buffer zone will be protected in terms of height controls and protection of viewsheds for examination by the World Heritage Centre and ICOMOS.
