The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  the report on the state of conservation of this property and further information provided by the Centre; \n 2.  Takes note  of the additional information presented by the State Party; \n 3.  Encourages  the State Party to continue its efforts to rehabilitate and enhance the state of conservation of this World Heritage property; \n 4.  Expresses  its appreciation to the State Party for welcoming the proposal for a joint UNESCO-ICOMOS reactive monitoring mission to examine the state of conservation of the property and to undertake consultations with the site management authorities; \n 5.  Requests  that a joint UNESCO-ICOMOS reactive monitoring mission with this mandate be organized before the 27th session of the Committee; \n 6.  Decides  to examine the findings and recommendations of this mission at its 27th session in June/July 2003.
