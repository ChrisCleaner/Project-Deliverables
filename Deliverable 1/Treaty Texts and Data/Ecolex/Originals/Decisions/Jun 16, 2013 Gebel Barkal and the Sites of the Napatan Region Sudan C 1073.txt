The World Heritage Committee,   \n 1.&nbsp;   Having examined   Document WHC-13/37.COM/7B.Add, \n 2.&nbsp;   Recalling   Decision   35 COM 7B.57   , adopted at its 35th&nbsp;session (UNESCO, 2011), \n 3.&nbsp;   Regrets   that the State Party did not submit neither the report nor detailed topographical maps of the five component parts of the property, as requested; \n 4.&nbsp;   Expresses its concern   about the absence of information on the state of conservation of the property and about the implementation of the recommendations of the 2011 mission; \n 5.&nbsp;   Urges   the State Party to implement the actions requested in paragraphs 4, 5, and 6 of Decision   35&nbsp;COM&nbsp;7B.57   , adopted at its 35th session (UNESCO, 2011); \n 6.&nbsp;   Reiterates its request   to the State Party to provide, in the framework of the Retrospective Inventory, detailed topographical maps of the five component parts of the property by   1&nbsp;December&nbsp;2013;   \n 7.&nbsp;   Also reiterates its request   to the State Party to finalize the limits of the buffer zones and their associated planning controls as soon as possible, to ensure that pressure from tourism, urban and infrastructure development do not have a negative impact on the Outstanding Universal Value of the property, and to submit a minor boundary modification by   1&nbsp;February&nbsp;2014   for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 38th&nbsp;session in 2014; \n 8.&nbsp;   Encourages   the State Party to benefit from the Sudan-Qatar Archaeological Project (QSAP) in order to address the above issues and   requests   it to keep the World Heritage Centre and the Advisory Bodies informed of any major conservation, restoration and development projects related to the property; \n 9.&nbsp;   Also requests   the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by   1&nbsp;February&nbsp;2014   , an updated report on the state of conservation of the property and the implementation of the above, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 38th&nbsp;session in 2014.
