The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Documents  WHC-09/33.COM/8B  and  WHC-09/33.COM/INF.8B1.Add,   \n 2.  Decides not to approve  the proposed minor modification to the boundary of the  Aachen Cathedral, Germany , and  considers  that the current proposal has a significant impact on the extent of the property and affects its outstanding universal value; \n 3. Decides not to approve the proposed name change from Aachen Cathedral to Aachen Cathedral with the Carolingian Palace Complex.
