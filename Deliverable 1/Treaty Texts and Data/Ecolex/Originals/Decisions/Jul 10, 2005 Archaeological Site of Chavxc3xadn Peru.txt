The World Heritage Committee, \n 1. Having examined Document   WHC-05/29.COM/7B.Rev  , \n 2. Recalling its Decision  28 COM 15B.104  adopted at its 28th session (Suzhou, 2004), \n 3. Taking note of the information provided by the State Party, \n 4. Urges the State Party of Peru to develop a management plan for the property of Chav&iacute;n, including La Banda, which should include statutory provisions for archaeological evaluation of all areas in and around the World Heritage property in advance of any form of intervention; \n 5. Requests the State Party to send an outline of a management plan to the World Heritage Centre by  1 February 2006  for examination by the Committee at its 30th session (Vilnius, 2006).
