The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;   Having examined   Document WHC-13/37.COM/7B, \n 2.&nbsp;   Recalling   Decision     36 COM 7B.81     , adopted at its 36th&nbsp;session (Saint-Petersburg, 2012), \n 3.&nbsp;   Takes note   of the conclusions and recommendations of the joint World Heritage Centre/ICOMOS/IUCN reactive monitoring mission, and   thanks   the Director-General of UNESCO for endorsing the recommendations of the mission; \n 4.&nbsp;   Notes with satisfaction   the comprehensive documentation provided by the State Party in response to the mission&rsquo;s recommendations; \n 5.&nbsp;   Requests   the State Party to continue to implement the recommendations of the joint reactive monitoring mission regarding the Foz Tua Hydro-Electric Dam project and in particular to: \n a)&nbsp; Provide the Environmental Impact Assessment for the high voltage transmission lines by   1 September 2013   to the World Heritage Centre, for review by the Advisory Bodies, before any decision on their trajectory is taken, \n b)&nbsp; Suspend further excavation of the navigation channel until hydraulic studies have been finalized and demonstrate that its lay-out is satisfactory in respect to its impact on the flow of the River Douro; \n 6.&nbsp;   Also requests   the State Party to submit the revised World Heritage Management Plan of the Alto Douro Wine Region by   1 February 2014   for review by the World Heritage Centre and the Advisory Bodies; \n 7.&nbsp;   Further requests   the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by   1 February 2015,   an updated report on the state of conservation of the property and the implementation of the above.
