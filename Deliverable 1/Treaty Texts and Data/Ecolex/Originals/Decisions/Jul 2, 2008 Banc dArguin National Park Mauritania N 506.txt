The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document  WHC-08/32.COM/7B.Add , \n 2.  Recalling  Decision  31 COM 7B.12 , adopted at its 31st session (Christchurch, 2007), \n 3.  Regrets  that the State Party did not submit a state of conservation report as requested at its 31 session (Christchurch, 2007); \n 4.  Welcomes  the findings of the IUCN Management Effectiveness Assessment on the positive progress in managing the property; but  notes with concern  the many threats which the property continues to face; \n 5.  Requests  the State Party to implement the recommendations of its previous decisions; and in particular to report on the following issues: \n a) monitoring the status of values of the property; \n b) protection of marine resources from overexploitation and pollution; and \n c) status of the proposed Oil spill emergency response plan; \n 6.  Also requests  the State Party to provide to the World Heritage Centre by  1 February 2009  a report on the state of conservation of the property, including progress in implementing the previous recommendations, together with a copy of the management plan for the next period, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 33rd session in 2009.
