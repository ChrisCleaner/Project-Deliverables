A.2 TRAINING  \n  A.2.5 ICCROM: Regional course for Latin America the Caribbean on Scientific Principles Conservation (US$ 30,000 requested)  \n The Bureau  approved  an amount of  US$ 30,000  as a contribution towards the costs of the regional course for Latin America and the Caribbean on Scientific Principles of Conservation, in order to improve the understanding of the elementary principles of scientific conservation relevant to different materials, the deterioration processes they undergo and the governing principles of different conservation/restoration treatments.
