The World Heritage Committee,  1.  Notes  the new information provided by the State Party;  2.  Urges  the State Party to proceed with the appointment of a non-governmental organization (NGO) representative to the Alligator Rivers Region Technical Committee (ARRTC);  3.  Requests  that the State Party provide a report on:   (a) progress on compliance with ISO14001,   (b) the ARRTC\'s continuing monitoring and review of water management and other environmental issues at Jabiluka and Ranger,   (c) consultation with the Traditional Owners of Kakadu on cultural heritage management and protection.n;  The report should be provided to the World Heritage Centre by 1 February 2004 in order that the World Heritage Committee can examine the state of conservation of the property at its 28th session in 2004.
