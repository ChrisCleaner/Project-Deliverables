The World Heritage Committee, \n \n  Having examined  Document WHC-15/39.COM/7B.Add, \n  Recalling  Decision  37 COM 8B.30 , adopted at its 37th session (Phnom Penh, 2013), \n  Acknowledges  the steps taken by the State Party to respond to the recommendations, notably the holding of a training workshop on tourism management in cooperation with UNESCO Beijing; \n  Encourages  the State Party to proceed with the development, adoption and implementation of the tourism management plan and interpretation plans, in close cooperation with UNESCO Beijing, the World Heritage Centre and the Advisory Bodies; \n  Requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 December 2016 , an updated report, including a 1-page executive summary, on the state of conservation of the property and the implementation of the above, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 41st session in 2017.
