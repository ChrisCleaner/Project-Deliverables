The World Heritage Committee,\n1. Taking note of the results of the evaluation mission carried out at the property,\nthe actions undertaken by the World Heritage Centre concerning the\ndemolition of the replica of the Gor\xc3\xa9e-Almadies Memorial, and the efforts\nundertaken by the Senegalese authorities to improve the state of conservation\nof the Island of Gor\xc3\xa9e,\n2. Considering that the erection of the replica of the Gor\xc3\xa9e-Almadies Memorial\nrefers to information communicated by the State Party during the 136th session\nof the Executive Board of UNESCO in 1991,\n3. Expresses its concern in view of the information regarding the erection of the\nreplica of the Gor\xc3\xa9e-Almadies Memorial on the property and its negative\nimpact on the integrity of the property;\n4. Invites the State Party to respond to the World Heritage Centre\'s letter of the\n10 December 2003 concerning the erection of the replica of the Gor\xc3\xa9e-\nAlmadies Memorial, requesting it to undertake corrective measures;\n5. Emphasizes the need to undertake urgent measures to improve the overall\nmanagement of the property, especially the elaboration of administrative\nprovisions for the nomination of a manager for the property who will be\nresponsible for the elaboration of a management plan;\n6. Invites the international community to support the funding of the project for\nthe protection of the coastal zone of the Island of Gor\xc3\xa9e;\n7. Requests the State Party to submit, by 1 February 2005, a report on the\nstrategies which will be put in place with a view to taking corrective measures\nto address the negative impact of the replica on the property, for examination\nby the Committee during its 29th session in 2005.
