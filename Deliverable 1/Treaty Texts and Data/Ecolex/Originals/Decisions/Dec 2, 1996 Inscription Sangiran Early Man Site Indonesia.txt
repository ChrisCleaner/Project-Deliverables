Sangiran Early Man Site   \n  593   \n  Indonesia    \n  C (iii) (vi)   \n The Committee decided to inscribe the nominated site under cultural criteria (iii) and (vi) as one of the key sites for the understanding of human evolution that admirably illustrates the development of  Homo sapiens sapiens  from the Lower Pleistocene to the present through the outstanding fossil and artefactual material that it has produced. \n &nbsp;
