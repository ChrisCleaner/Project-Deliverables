The World Heritage Committee, \n \n  Having examined  Document WHC-14/38.COM/7B, \n  Recalling  Decision  36 COM 7B.57 , adopted at its 36th session (Saint-Petersburg, 2012), \n  Commends  the State Party for the efforts aiming at ensuring the long-term management and conservation of the property, and the launching of the project entitled \xe2\x80\x9cRehabilitating and Refurbishing the Old Souq at Bahla: From Study to Reinstatement\xe2\x80\x9d, and of the monitoring of the aging characteristics of the different brick types; \n  Regrets  that neither the finalized version of the management plan nor a request for a minor boundary modification to enlarge the buffer zone have been submitted; \n  Requests  the State Party to submit the finalized version of the management plan, including the legal framework that will support its implementation, to the World Heritage Centre as soon as possible; \n  Also requests  the State Party to submit, by  1 February 2015 , a request for a minor boundary modification in view of enlarging the buffer zone, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 39th session in 2015; \n  Further requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 December 2015 , an updated report, including a 1-page executive summary, on the state of conservation of the property and the results of the monitoring of the aging characteristics of the different brick types, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 40th session in 2016.
