Property:  Desembarco del Granma National Park  \n Id. N\xc2\xb0:  889  \n State Party:  Cuba    \n Criteria:  N (i) (iii)  \n The uplifted marine terraces of the Desembarco del Granma National Park and associated ongoing development of karst topography and features, represent a globally significant example of geomorphologic and physiographic features and ongoing geological processes. The area includes spectacular stair-step terraces and cliffs and the ecosystems that have evolved on them, as well as some of the most pristine and impressive coastal cliffs bordering the Western Atlantic between the Canadian Maritimes and southern South America. \n The Committee decided to inscribe the site under natural  criteria (i)  and  (iii) . It also commended the Government of Cuba for the efforts to conserve this site. The Committee suggested that the State Party submit a request to the World Heritage Fund for technical assistance to produce a tourism management plan as an integral element of the overall management plan for this site.
