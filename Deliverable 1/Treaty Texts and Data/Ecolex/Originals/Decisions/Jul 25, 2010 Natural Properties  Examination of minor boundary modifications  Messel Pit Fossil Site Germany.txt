The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.    Having examined  Documents WHC-10/34.COM/8B and WHC-10/34.COM/INF.8B2, \n 2.    Approves  the proposed creation of a 22.5 ha buffer zone for the 42 ha  Messel Pit Fossil Site, Germany  in order to strengthen the integrity of the inscribed property and support its effective protection and management;&nbsp; \n 3.
