The Conference of the Parties   1.  Adopts  the revised Terms of Reference of the Working Group on Sustainable Industry, Energy, Transport and Infrastructure, and calls for the development of a Protocol on Sustainable Industry, Energy, Transport and Infrastructure;  2.  Takes note  of the initiative to create a "Sustainable Energy Research Alliance" and requests the Secretariat to report on developments in this matter;  3.  Encourages  future cooperation activities with the United Nations Industrial Development Organization, the Food and Agriculture Organization and other relevant organizations in the field of renewable energies in the Carpathians.
