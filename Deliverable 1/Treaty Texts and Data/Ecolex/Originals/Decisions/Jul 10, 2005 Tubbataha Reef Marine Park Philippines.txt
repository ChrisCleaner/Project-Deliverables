The World Heritage Committee, \n 1. Having examined Document   WHC-05/29.COM/7B.Rev  , \n 2. Recalling its Decision  28 COM 15B.18 , adopted at its 28th session (Suzhou, 2004), \n 3. Notes with satisfaction that the State Party of Philippines has begun plans and sought international assistance to organise a Sub-regional Workshop on Illegal Fishing in the Sulu Sea; \n 4. Urges the State Party to work with the World Heritage Centre and IUCN to further develop and refine these plans for the Workshop and submit a reformulated international assistance request so that the Chairperson of the Committee may consider its approval at the earliest possible time; \n 5. Requests the State Party to provide to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 February 2006,  a report on the implementation of the recommendations contained in Decision  28 COM 15B.18 , for examination by the Committee at its 30th session (Vilnius, 2006).
