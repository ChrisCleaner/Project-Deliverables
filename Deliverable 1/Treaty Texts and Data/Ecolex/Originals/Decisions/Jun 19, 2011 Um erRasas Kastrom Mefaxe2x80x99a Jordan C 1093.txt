The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document WHC-11/35.COM/7B, \n 2.  Recalling  Decision  33 COM   7B.56 , adopted at its 33rd session (Seville, 2009), \n 3.  Requests  the State Party to finalize a scientifically and technically sound conservation and restoration project for the Stylite tower and to submit it a soon as possible to the World Heritage Centre, for review by the Advisory Bodies, prior to implementation; \n 4.  Urges  the State Party to complete the management plan for the property, including a comprehensive conservation plan, as well as an archaeological research policy and a public use plan; \n 5.  Also requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 February 2013 , a progress report on the implementation of the above, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 37th session in 2013.
