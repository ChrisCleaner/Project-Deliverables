The  Seventh Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. VII/30:      That the amount of controlled substances produced and exported for the purpose of being entirely used as feedstock in the manufacture of other chemicals in importing countries should not be the subject of the calculation of \xe2\x80\x9cproduction\xe2\x80\x9d or \xe2\x80\x9cconsumption\xe2\x80\x9d in exporting countries. Importers shall, prior to export, provide exporters with a commitment that the controlled substances imported shall be used for this purpose. In addition, importing countries shall report to the Secretariat on the volumes of controlled substances imported for these purposes;   That the amount of controlled substances entirely used as feedstock in the manufacture of other chemicals should not be the subject of calculation of \xe2\x80\x9cconsumption\xe2\x80\x9d in importing countries.
