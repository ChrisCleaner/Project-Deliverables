Property:  The Old City of Mostar  \n Id. N&deg;:  946  \n State Party:  Bosnia and Herzegovina    \n Criteria:  C (iv) (vi)  \n ICOMOS recommended that this property be inscribed under criteria (iv) and (vi). However, following information received from the UNESCO Office in Sarajevo concerning the threats to the site due to uncontrolled building in the old town and its perimeter, the Committee decided to defer the inscription of this property, in order to allow the State Party to provide more information on the protection of the site. Furthermore, the Committee requested the Secretariat to report on this issue at the forthcoming session of the Bureau. \n &nbsp;
