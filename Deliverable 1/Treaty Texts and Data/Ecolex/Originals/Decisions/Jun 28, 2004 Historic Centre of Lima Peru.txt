The World Heritage Committee,\n1. Taking note of the reports transmitted by the State Party (Decision 27 COM\n7B.99),\n2. Requests the State Party to formalize the current procedures to set up a\nManagement Coordination Unit to implement the Strategic Plan with full\nresponsibility for implementing the projects, composed of all the relevant\ninstitutions for the protection and development of the Historical Centre of\nLima;\n3. Draws the attention of the State Party to the possibility of organizing an\ninternational expert meeting to improve civil participation and private\ninvestment in the management project;\n4. Further requests the State Party to keep the Committee informed of the further\nimplementation of the Strategic Plan and Management Plan for the Historical\nCentre of Lima.
