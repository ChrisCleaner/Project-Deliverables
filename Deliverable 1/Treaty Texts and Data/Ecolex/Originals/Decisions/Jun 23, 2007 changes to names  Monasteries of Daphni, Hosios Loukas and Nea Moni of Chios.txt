The World Heritage Committee,    Having examined  Document WHC-07/31.COM/8B ,    Approves  the proposed name change to Monasteries of Daphni, Hossios Luckas and Nea Moni of Chios as proposed by the Greek authorities. The name of the property becomes  Monasteries of Daphni, Hosios Loukas and Nea Moni of Chios  in English and  Monast\xc3\xa8res de Daphni, de Hosios Loukas et Nea Moni de Chios  in French
