Property:  The Holy Trinity Column in Olomouc  \n Id. N&deg;:  859 Rev   \n State Party:  Czech   Republic     \n Criteria:  C (i) (iv)   \n The Committee decided to inscribe this property on the World Heritage List on the basis of  criteria (i) and (iv):  \n  Criterion (i) : The Olomouc Holy Trinity Column is one of the most exceptional examples of the apogee of central European Baroque artistic expression. \n  Criterion (iv) : The Holy Trinity Column constituted a unique material demonstration of religious faith in central Europe during the Baroque period, and the Olomouc example represents its most outstanding expression. \n The Delegate of Greece expressed some reservations regarding the application of criterion  (i)  for this site. \n &nbsp;
