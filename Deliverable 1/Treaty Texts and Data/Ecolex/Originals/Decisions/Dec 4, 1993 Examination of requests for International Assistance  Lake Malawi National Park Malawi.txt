A. NATURAL HERITAGE \n  A.1&nbsp; Requests on which the Bureau took a decision  \n A.1.2&nbsp; Technical co-operation \n  \n  A.1.2.1&nbsp; Technical assistance request for Lake Malawi National Park (Malawi)  \n The Bureau  approved  a technical assistance request for a total of  US$ 16,000  subject to the Government of Malawi making full payment of its dues to the World Heritage Fund. The project involves the purchase of equipment (boats, tents, diving equipment, life jackets and binoculars) for Lake Malawi National Park. \n &nbsp;
