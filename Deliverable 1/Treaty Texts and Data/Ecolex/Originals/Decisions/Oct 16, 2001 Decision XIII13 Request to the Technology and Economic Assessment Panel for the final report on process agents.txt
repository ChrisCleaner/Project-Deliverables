The  Thirteenth Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. XIII/13:     Noting  with appreciation the report of the Executive Committee in response to decision X/14 on process agents,    Noting  the findings of the Technology and Economic Assessment Panel and its request for additional data for the finalization of its report,    Noting  that in 2001 Parties provided the Ozone Secretariat with the requested additional data,   To request the Technology and Economic Assessment Panel to finalize its evaluation, as requested by decision X/14, and report to the Parties at the 22nd Meeting of the Open-ended Working Group, in 2002.
