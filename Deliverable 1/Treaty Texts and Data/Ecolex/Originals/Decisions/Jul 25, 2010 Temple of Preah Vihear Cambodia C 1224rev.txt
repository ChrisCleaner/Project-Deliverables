The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp; Having  received Document WHC-10/34.COM/7B.Add.3, \n 2.&nbsp; Recalling  Decisions  31   COM 8B.24, 32 COM 8B.102 , and  33 COM   7B.65,  adopted at its 31st session (Christchurch, 2007), 32nd session (Quebec City, 2008), and 33rd session (Seville, 2009) respectively, \n 3.&nbsp; Takes note  that the World Heritage Centre has the documents submitted by the State Party; \n 4.&nbsp; Further welcomes  the steps taken by the State Party towards the establishment of an international coordinating committee for the sustainable conservation of the Temple of Preah Vihear; \n 5.&nbsp; Decides  to consider the documents submitted by the State Party at its 35th session in 2011.
