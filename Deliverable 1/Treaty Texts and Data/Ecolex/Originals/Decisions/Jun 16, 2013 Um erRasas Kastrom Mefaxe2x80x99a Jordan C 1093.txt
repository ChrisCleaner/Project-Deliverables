The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;   Having examined   Document WHC-13/37.COM/7B, \n 2.&nbsp;   Recalling   Decision     35 COM         7B.50     , adopted at its 35th&nbsp;session (UNESCO, 2011), \n 3.&nbsp;   Takes note   of the progress in the implementation of conservation and monitoring measures at the Stylite tower and   requests   the State Party to provide additional technical details about these measures to the World Heritage Centre, for review by the Advisory Bodies; \n 4.&nbsp;   Urges   the State Party to complete the management plan which must include a comprehensive conservation plan as well as an archaeological research policy and a public use plan; \n 5.&nbsp;   Also requests   the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by   1&nbsp;February&nbsp;2015   , a progress report on the implementation of the above, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 39th session in 2015.
