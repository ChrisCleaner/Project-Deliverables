xc2\xa0  The World Heritage Committee,  1.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Having examined  Document WHC-07/31.COM/7A,  2.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Recalling  Decision  30 COM 7A.23 , adopted at its 30th   session (Vilnius, 2006),   3.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Notes with satisfaction  the efforts and commitment of the State Party and the international community for the safeguarding of this property;   4.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Adopts  the following as the desired state of conservation for the property in view of its future removal from the List of World Heritage in Danger:  a)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 ensured site security,  b)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 ensured long-term stability of the Giant Buddha niches,   c)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 adequate state of conservation of archaeological remains and mural paintings, and  d)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 implemented Management Plan and Cultural Master Plan (the protective zoning plan);  5.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Encourages  the State Party to implement corrective measures for:  a)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 ensured site security by   (i)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 exerting strict control of illicit excavations and looting through hiring of adequate number of trained site guards, and   (ii)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 clearing unexploded ordinances and anti-personnel mines from the property;   b)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 ensured long-term stability of the Giant Buddha niches by installing a permanent monitoring system;   c)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 adequate state of conservation of archaeological remains and mural paintings by   (i)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 completing the conservation of the fragments of the Giant Buddha statues and   (ii)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 completing the conservation of the mural paintings in the prioritized buddhist caves;   d)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 the Management Plan and the Cultural Master Plan (the protective zoning plan) both implemented by developing institutional capacity, notably for the Ministry of Culture and the intersectoral Bamiyan Cultural Landscape Coordination Committee (BCLCC);  6.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Invites  the international community to continue its technical and financial support, in particular to achieve the above indicated desired state of conservation;   7.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Requests  the State Party, in consultation with the World Heritage Centre and ICOMOS, to develop a draft Statement of Outstanding Universal Value including the conditions of integrity and authenticity, for examination by the Committee at its 32nd session in 2008;   8.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Also requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre by  1 February 2008  the Management Plan and a progress report on the implementation of corrective measures, for examination by the Committee at its 32nd session in 2008; and  9. \xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Decides  to retain the Cultural Landscape and Archaeological Remains of the Bamiyan Valley (Afghanistan) on the List of World Heritage in Danger.
