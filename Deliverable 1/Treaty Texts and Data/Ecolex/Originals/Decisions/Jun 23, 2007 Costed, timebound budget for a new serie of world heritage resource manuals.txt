The World Heritage Committee,\n \n  Having examined  Document WHC-07/31.COM/21B, \n  Recalling  Decisions  30 COM 6  and  30 COM 15.2,  adopted at its 30th session (Vilnius, 2006); \n \xc2\xa0 Endorses  the proposed list of titles for the new World Heritage Resource Manuals Series, as well as the outlined timeline; \n  Thanks  the Netherlands Funds-in-Trust for their financial assistance in the production of the Resource Manual on Management Guidelines for Cultural Landscapes; \n  Approves  the budget for the following titles to be published in 2008/2009 (see Decision  31 COM 20B ):\n \n Guidance on Preparing World Heritage Nominations, \n Preparing Management Plans for Natural World Heritage Properties, \n Preparing Management Plans for Cultural World Heritage Properties, and \n Tourism and World Heritage Resource Manual \n \n \n  Requests  the World Heritage Centre, in consultation with the Advisory Bodies, to provide the titles and costs of proposed future manuals, for examination by the Committee at its 32nd session in 2008.
