The World Heritage Committee,   1.  Having examined  Document  WHC-06/30.COM/8B ,   2.  Approves  the proposed name change to La Fortaleza and San Juan Historic Site in Puerto Rico as proposed by the American authorities. The name of the property becomes  La Fortaleza and San Juan National Historic Site in Puerto Rico  in English and  La Fortaleza et le site historique national de San Juan \xc3\xa0 Porto Rico  in French.
