The World Heritage Committee, \n \n  Having examined  Document WHC-14/38.COM/7A, \n  Recalling  Decision  37 COM 7A.32 , adopted at its 37th session (Phnom Penh, 2013), \n  Welcomes  the progress in the implementation of the conservation programme plan for Gelati Monastery; \n  Notes  that the State Party submitted on 1 February 2014 a request for a Major Boundary Modification for the property, further to the request of the Committee; \n   Decides    to retain the Bagrati Cathedral and Gelati Monastery (Georgia) on the List of World Heritage in Danger.
