The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Documents WHC-10/34.COM/8B and WHC-10/34.COM/INF.8B1.Add, \n 2.  Approves  the buffer zone for the  Cathedral, Alc&aacute;zar and Archivo de Indias in Seville, Spain.  \n 3.  Notes  that the City Council has agreed to complete the remaining catalogues for the sectors within the buffer zone and  urges  the State Party to ensure that these are in place as soon as possible; \n 4.  Also notes  that development outside the buffer zone in the wider setting will be subject to impact assessments on the inscribed property under the 2007 Historical Heritage Act and  also urges  the State Party to ensure that these are applied rigorously. \n &nbsp;
