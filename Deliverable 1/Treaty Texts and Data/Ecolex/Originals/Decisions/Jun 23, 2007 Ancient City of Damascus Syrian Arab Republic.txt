The World Heritage Committee,   1.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Having examined  Document WHC-07/31.COM/7B.Add,  2.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Notes with satisfaction  that the State Party has prepared an Action Plan for the conservation of the property;   3.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Encourages  the State Party to implement the Action Plan provided that:  a)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Any ring road around the property must respect the integrity of the property;  b)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Infrastructure works are planned and implemented under high quality archaeological supervision;  c)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 The legal framework for the protection of the property is improved and detailed;  d)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 The use of traditional restoration techniques within the property becomes compulsory to guarantee the preservation of the property\'s integrity;  4.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Urges  the State Party to refrain from undertaking any demolition work within the property and its buffer zone, in case such work impacts on the integrity of the property;  5.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Invites  the State Party to consider extending the boundaries of the property in order to include its valuable historical neighbourhoods and  requests  the State Party to define the boundaries of the proposed buffer zone and to officially provide a map of this zone to the World Heritage Centre for approval by the Committee;  6.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Requests  the State Party, in accordance with Paragraph 172 of the  Operational Guidelines , to inform the World Heritage Centre in advance of any planned changes and any foreseen project within and around the property;  7.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Further requests  the State Party to invite a joint World Heritage Centre/ICOMOS mission in order to assess the situation and to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 February 2008 , a progress report on the above recommendations and on the state of conservation of the property for examination by the Committee at its 32nd session in 2008.
