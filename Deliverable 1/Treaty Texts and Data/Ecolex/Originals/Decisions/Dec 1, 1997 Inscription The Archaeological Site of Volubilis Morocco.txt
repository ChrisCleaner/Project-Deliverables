The Archaeological Site of Volubilis  \n  836   \n  Morocco     \n  C(ii)(iii)(iv)(vi)   \n The Committee decided to inscribe the Archaeological Site of Volubilis on the basis of criteria (ii), (iii), (iv) and (vi), considering that this site is an exceptionally well preserved example of a large Roman colonial town on the fringes of the Empire. \n &nbsp;
