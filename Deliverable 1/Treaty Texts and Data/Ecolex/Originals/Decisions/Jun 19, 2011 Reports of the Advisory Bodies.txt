The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp; Having examined  Document WHC-11/35.COM/5B, \n 2.&nbsp; Takes note  of the reports of the Advisory Bodies (ICCROM, ICOMOS and IUCN) on their activities and  expresses its gratitude  for the efforts towards the implementation of the  Convention ; \n 3.&nbsp; Requests  the Advisory Bodies for the next sessions in order to take informed and transparent decisions: \n a) to harmonize their reports, indicating the human and financial resources committed by the Advisory Bodies in the different activities they are in charge of, \n b) to include a general joint comment on the progress made and gaps identified for the implementation of the  Convention , \n c) to present a joint summary table with the World Heritage Centre on their commitments and activities.
