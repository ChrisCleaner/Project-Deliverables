The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Having examined  Document WHC-12/36.COM/9B; \n 2.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Recalling  Decisions  34 COM 9C  and  35 COM 9B  adopted at its 34th (Brasilia, 2010) and 35th (UNESCO, 2011) sessions respectively; \n 3.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Takes note  of the progress made on the implementation of the World Heritage Capacity Building Strategy (WHCBS) by a variety of capacity building stakeholders; \n 4.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Thanks  the State Party of Switzerland for its continued support to the on-going IUCN-ICCROM World Heritage Capacity Building Initiative; \n 5.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Welcomes  the proposed new international capacity building programme; \n 6.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Requests  the Advisory Bodies, the World Heritage Centre, and the UNESCO Category 2 Centres and relevant UNESCO Chairs to assist States Parties in the development of regional capacity building programmes which are based on the outcomes of the Periodic Reporting exercises and the WHCBS; \n 7.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Requests  the World Heritage Centre, in coordination with the Advisory Bodies and Category 2 centres, to continue providing an inventory of heritage training centres in all regions with a view to sharing experience and expertise; \n 8.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Encourages  States Parties and the international community to support both the international capacity building programme and the regional capacity building programmes as they are developed and put into implementation; \n 9.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Also requests  ICCROM, in consultation with IUCN, ICOMOS and the World Heritage Centre, to report on the progress made in the implementation of the World Heritage Capacity Building Strategy (WHCBS) for examination by the Committee at its 37th session in 2013.\xc2\xa0
