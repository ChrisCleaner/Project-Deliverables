The  Fourth Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. IV/29:      to note with appreciation the report: \xe2\x80\x9cMeeting of the needs of Article 5 Parties for controlled substances during the grace and phase-out periods\xe2\x80\x9d, prepared by the Executive Committee of the Interim Multilateral Fund for the Implementation of the Montreal Protocol;   to request the Executive Committee to update its report and submit it to the Seventh Meeting of the Parties to the Montreal Protocol, in 1995, through the Secretariat, before 31 December 1994;   to request Parties to take note of the Executive Committee\xe2\x80\x99s report and to take the necessary steps, consistent with the provisions of the Protocol, to promote an adequate supply of controlled substances in order to meet the needs of the Parties operating under paragraph 1 of Article 5 of the Protocol.
