Thungyai-Huai Kha Khaeng Wildlife Sanctuaries  \n  591  \n  Thailand  \n  N(ii)(iii) (iv)  \n The Committee encouraged the authorities of Thailand to accelerate the implementation of management plans for the two Sanctuaries. The Committee complimented Thailand for rejecting the proposal for the construction of the Nam Choan Dam. The Committee observed that it would be concerned over any proposal that might affect the integrity of adjacent forests in Myanmar. The Committee noted that the Government of Myanmar may nominate these adjacent forests for inscription on the World Heritage List when it becomes a State Party to the Convention. \n &nbsp;
