The World Heritage Committee,  1.  Acknowledges  the support provided by the State Party to produce the  Ngorongoro Crater Ungulate Study , while requesting the State Party to implement its recommendations;  2.  Urges  the State Party to consider imposing a moratorium on new development in and around the Crater (Crater Highlands), until the impacts of the current lodges, road system and other tourism developments are assessed, with particular emphasis on water usage;\xc2\xa0  3.  Requests  the State Party submit a report on the state of conservation of the site, including its response to the  Ngorongoro Crater Ungulate Study , by 1 February 2003   for examination at its 27th session in June/July 2003.
