The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Having examined  Document WHC-12/36.COM/12D; \n 2.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Takes note  of the progress made in the preparation of the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the  World Heritage Convention  under the theme &ldquo;World Heritage and Sustainable Development: the Role of Local Communities&rdquo;; \n 3.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Encourages  States Parties to continue to develop, support and carry out activities to promote the anniversary; \n 4.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Invites  the World Heritage Centre to continue implementing the programme of activities assisted by extrabudgetary funding available or to be identified; \n 5.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Requests  the World Heritage Centre to report on the 40th anniversary celebrations to the 37th session of the World Heritage Committee (2013).
