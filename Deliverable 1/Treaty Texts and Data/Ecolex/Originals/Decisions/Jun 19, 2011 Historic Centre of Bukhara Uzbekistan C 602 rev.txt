The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document WHC-11/35.COM/7B.Add, \n 2.  Recalling  Decision  34 COM 7B.74 , adopted at its 34th session (Brasilia, 2010), \n 3.  Notes  the results of the October 2010 joint World Heritage Centre/ICOMOS reactive monitoring mission and the identified threats to the property; \n 4.  Urges  the State Party to undertake, in a timely fashion, the measures recommended by the October 2010 mission report, particularly the need to complete and implement the Management Plan and the establishment of the Bukhara World Heritage Steering Committee for the property; \n 5.  Requests  the State Party to address potential negative impacts on authenticity and integrity of the property to ensure the protection of its Outstanding Universal Value; \n 6.  Also requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 February 2013 , a report on the progress made in implementing the recommendations of the October 2010 reactive monitoring mission, and the state of conservation of the property, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 37th session in 2013.
