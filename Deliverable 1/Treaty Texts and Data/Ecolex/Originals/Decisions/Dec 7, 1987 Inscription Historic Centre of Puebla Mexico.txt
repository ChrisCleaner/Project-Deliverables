Historic Centre of Puebla  \n  416  \n  Mexico  \n  C(ii)(iv)  \n The Committee considered that the complementarity of the colonial city of Puebla and of the Pre-Hispanic site of Cholula had been weakened by the urbanization of the area and that it was therefore not possible in the present circumstances to proceed with a joint inscription.
