The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Taking into account    Rule 21 of the Rules of Procedure of the World Heritage Committee, on the creation of Subsidiary bodies, \n 2.  Establishes  a Working Group on Item 15 (Budget) of the Agenda of its 35th session as a Subsidiary Body, that shall meet for the duration of the 35th session, with a specific mandate to: \n a) Examine item 15 of the Agenda and to formulate its recommendations thereon; and \n b) Report back to the Committee at its plenary session.
