The World Heritage Committee [17],  1.  Noting  the actions undertaken so far by the World Heritage Centre and the Advisory Bodies to improve and assist Bandiagara,;  2.  Considering  the importance to undertake all necessary measures for preventive action to protect a property inscribed on the World Heritage List,;  3.
