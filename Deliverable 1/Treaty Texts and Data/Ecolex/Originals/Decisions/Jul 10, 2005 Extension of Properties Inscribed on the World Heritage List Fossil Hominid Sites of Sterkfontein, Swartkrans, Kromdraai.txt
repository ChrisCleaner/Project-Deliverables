The World Heritage Committee,  1.  Having examined  Documents   WHC-05/29.COM/8B   and   WHC-    05/29.COM/INF.8B.1  ,  2.  Approves  the extension of the  Fossil Hominid Sites of Sterkfontein,   Swartkrans, Kromdraai, and Environs  (South Africa) to include Makapan Valley and Taung Skull Fossil Site, on the basis of the existing criteria (iii) and (vi).  3.  Notes  that the State Party of South Africa is in the process of finalizing the new name for the serial property.
