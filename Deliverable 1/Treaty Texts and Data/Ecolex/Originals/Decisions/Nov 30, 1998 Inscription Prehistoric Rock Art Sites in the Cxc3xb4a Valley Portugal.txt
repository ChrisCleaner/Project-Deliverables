N&deg;:  866   \n State Party:  Portugal     \n Criteria:  C (i)(iii)   \n The Committee inscribed the site on the World Heritage List on the basis of  criteria (i) and (iii) : \n  Criterion (i) : The Upper Palaeolithic rock-art of the C&ocirc;a valley is an outstanding example of the sudden flowering of creative genius at the dawn of human cultural development. \n  Criterion (iii) : The C&ocirc;a Valley rock art throws light on the social, economic, and spiritual life on the life of the early ancestor of humankind in a wholly exceptional manner. \n The Delegate of Thailand agreed with the inscription but expressed his reservation with the application of criterion (i).
