The Conference of the Parties   1.  decides  to accept the generous offer of the Government of the Slovak Republic to convene the Third Meeting of the Conference of the Parties to the Carpathian Convention (COP3) in the Slovak Republic in 2011, and  requests  the interim Secretariat to make the necessary arrangements, in consultation with the host Government and the Bureau of the COP;  2.  requests  the interim Secretariat to undertake the necessary preparation in consultation with all interested partners and stakeholders;  3.  invites  all interested partners to make a financial contribution to the organization of the Third Meeting of the Conference of the Parties to the Carpathian Convention (COP3).
