The World Heritage Committee,&nbsp; \n 1.  Having examined  Document WHC-10/34.COM/7.1, \n 2.  Recalling  its Decision  33 COM 7.1 , adopted at its 33rd session (Seville, 2009), \n 3.  Thanks  the State Party of Brazil and IPHAN for having generously hosted the expert meeting which took place from 7 to 11 December 2009 in Rio de Janeiro (Brazil), and the experts having contributed to the meeting; \n 4.  Takes note with satisfaction  of the outcomes of the expert meeting and the recommendations for the inclusion of an Historic Urban Landscape approach in the  Operational Guidelines ; \n 5.  Requests  the World Heritage Centre to develop, in cooperation with the Advisory Bodies, a manual on the applications and case studies reflecting best practices of the Historic Urban Landscape approach; \n 6.  Further requests  the World Heritage Centre to inform the World Heritage Committee of progress in the development of this manual of best practices at its 35th session in 2011.
