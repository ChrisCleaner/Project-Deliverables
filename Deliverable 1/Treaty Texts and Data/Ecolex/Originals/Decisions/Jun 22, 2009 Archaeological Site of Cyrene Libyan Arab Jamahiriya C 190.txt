The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document  WHC-09/33.COM/7B Add , \n 2.  Recalling  Decision  31 COM 7B.63 , adoped at its 31st session (Christchurch, 2007), \n 3.  Regrets  that the State Party has not submitted a report on the state of conservation of the property and the implementation of its recommendations, nor a map indicating the boundaries of the property; \n 4.  Strongly urges  the State Party to implement the measures recommended by the joint World Heritage Centre/ICOMOS mission of January 2007 and its earlier decisions; \n 5.  Requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 February 2010 , a report on the state of conservation of the property and the implementation of its Decision  31 COM 7B.63 , for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 34th session in 2010.
