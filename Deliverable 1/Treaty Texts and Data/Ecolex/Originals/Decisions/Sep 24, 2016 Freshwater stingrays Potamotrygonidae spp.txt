Directed to range States of freshwater stingrays     Range States of freshwater stingrays (family Potamotrygonidae) are encouraged to include all species of concern*, as identified by the Animals Committee, in Appendix III, and consider options for including species in Appendix II.    *Species of priority concern as identified by the Animals Committee in the Annex to document AC28 Doc. 18:    \xe2\x80\x93 Paratrygon "aiereba" species complex (Amazon and Orinoco basins)    \xe2\x80\x93 Potamotrygon leopoldi (Amazon basin)    \xe2\x80\x93 Potamotrygon schroederi (Amazon and Orinoco basins)    \xe2\x80\x93 Potamotrygon brachyura (other catchment basins)    \xe2\x80\x93 Potamotrygon "motoro" species complex (all catchments)    Note that the priority species identified include both undescribed species and clusters of species.
