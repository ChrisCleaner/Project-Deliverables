The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Documents WHC-11/35.COM/8B.Add and WHC-11/35.COM/INF.8B1.Add, \n 2.  Approves  the proposed buffer zone for the  Old   City of Damascus, Syrian Arab Republic;  \n 3.  Recommends  that the State Party give consideration to the following points: \n a) Consider extending the buffer zone as currently defined so as to ensure a better connection between the historic quarters of the ancient city and the inscribed property, \n b) Continue the regulation and control efforts, either currently under way or announced, for the various parts of the buffer zone, and to keep the World Heritage Committee up to date with progress in this matter.
