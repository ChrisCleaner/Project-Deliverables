The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document  WHC-08/32.COM/6A , \n 2.  Recalling  Decisions  29 COM 11C.1, 30 COM 11F  and  31 COM 23B  adopted at its 29th (Durban, 2005), 30th (Vilnius, 2006),and at its 31st (Christchurch, 2007) sessions respectively; \n 3.  Takes note  of the Report of progress made in the implementation of the African World Heritage Fund; \n 4.  Expresses its appreciation  to partners at all levels for their support, and their technical and financial assistance to the African World Heritage Fund; \n 5.  Welcomes  the report of the first advocacy meeting organized in Abuja (Nigeria) from 24-25 April 2008; \n 6.  Notes  the delay in collecting the funds from partners who have committed contributions; \n 7.  Requests  the Board of Trustees to engage staff dedicated to resource mobilization, including follow-up of commitments; \n 8.  Calls upon  the State Parties to the  Convention , the African Union, the public and private institutions, the intergovernmental and non-governmental organizations, to continue supporting and contributing to the African World Heritage Fund; \n 9.  Appeals  to the international community to support the African World Heritage Fund and its funding strategy so as to ensure that the targeted Endowment Fund is quickly consolidated to enable the Fund becoming operational; \n 10.  Requests  the African World Heritage Fund to submit a detailed progress report both on the African World Heritage Fund\'s activities and on the strategy for future activities of capacity building for the preparation of nominations and of tentative lists, as well as for conservation and management plans of African properties, for examination at its 33rd session in 2009.
