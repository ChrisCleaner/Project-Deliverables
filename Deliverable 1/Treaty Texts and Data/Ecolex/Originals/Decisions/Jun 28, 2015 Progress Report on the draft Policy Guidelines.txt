The World Heritage Committee, \n \n  Having examined  Document WHC-15/39.COM/12, \n  Recalling  Decision  35 COM 12B  and Decision  37 COM 13  adopted respectively at its 35th session (UNESCO, 2011) and its 37th session (Phnom Penh, 2013); \n  Thanks  the Government of Australia for their commitment and financial contribution in view of the development of a Policy Guidelines document for a better implementation of the  World Heritage Convention ; \n  Takes note  of the status report prepared by the World Heritage Centre on the progress made so far; \n  Thanks  ICCROM for taking the lead for undertaking the scoping study in close consultation with ICOMOS and IUCN and the World Heritage Centre; \n  Requests  the World Heritage Centre and Advisory Bodies to make additional progress beyond the scoping study over the next year; \n  Requests  ICCROM and other stakeholders involved in the preparation of the scoping study to take into account the Policy Document on sustainable development \xe2\x80\x93 once the Policy Document is adopted by the General Assembly of the States Parties \xe2\x80\x93 as one of the essential elements to be inserted in the future Policy Guidelines; \n  Also requests  the World Heritage Centre to present the scoping study along with additional progress report to the World Heritage Committee at its 40th session in 2016.
