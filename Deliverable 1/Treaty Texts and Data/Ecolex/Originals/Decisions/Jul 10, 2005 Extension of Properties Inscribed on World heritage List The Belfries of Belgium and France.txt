The World Heritage Committee,  1.  Having examined  Documents   WHC-05/29.COM/8B, WHC-05/29.COM/8B.Add 2   and   WHC-05/29.COM/INF.8B.1  ,  2.  Approves  the extension of  The Belfries of Flanders and Wallonia  (Belgium) to include  The Belfries of Flanders, Artois, Hainaut and Picardy  (France), on the basis of the cultural criteria (ii) and (iv);  3.  Also approves  the minor modification to The Belfries of Flanders and Wallonia adding the Belfry in Gembloux, Belgium, on the basis of the existing criteria (ii) and (iv);  4.  Notes  the changing of the name of the property (including the Belfry in Gembloux), which as extended becomes: &quot; The Belfries of Belgium and   France &quot;;  5.  Decides  that the list of inscribed Belfries is hereupon closed.
