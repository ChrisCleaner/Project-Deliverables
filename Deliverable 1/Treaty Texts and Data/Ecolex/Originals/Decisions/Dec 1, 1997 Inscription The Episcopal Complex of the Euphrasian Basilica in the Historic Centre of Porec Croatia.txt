The Episcopal Complex of the Euphrasian Basilica in the Historic Centre of Porec  \n  809   \n  Croatia     \n  C(ii)(iii)(iv)   \n The Committee decided to inscribe this property on the basis of criteria (ii), (iii) and (iv), considering that the Episcopal complex of the Euphrasian Basilica in the historic centre of Porec is an outstanding example of an early Christian episcopal ensemble that is exceptional by virtue of its completeness and its unique Basilican cathedral. \n &nbsp;
