Sangay   National Park (Ecuador)  \n The site was inscribed in 1983 and added to the List of World Heritage in Danger in 1992 due to\' threats from poachers, boundary encroachment and unplanned road construction. The situation at the site was discussed between a representative of the President of Ecuador and World Heritage Centre staff and the Committee\'s continuous concerns were brought to the attention of the Government of Ecuador. \n The Committee decided to retain this site on the List of World Heritage in Danger and that another fact-finding mission should be carried out. \n &nbsp;
