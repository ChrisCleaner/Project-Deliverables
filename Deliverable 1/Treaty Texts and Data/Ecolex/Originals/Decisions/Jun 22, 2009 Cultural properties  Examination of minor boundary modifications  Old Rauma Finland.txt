The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Documents  WHC-09/33.COM/8B  and  WHC-09/33.COM/INF.8B1.Add , \n 2.  Approves  the proposed buffer zone for  Old Rauma ,  Finland ; \n 3.  Recommends  that the State Party give consideration to the following: \n a) The designation of viewpoints within and outside the buffer zone from which view corridors will be protected in the case of development within the buffer zone; \n b) The development of a comprehensive Management Plan to cover the nominated area, the buffer zone, and view corridors from any designated locations outside the buffer zone, such as the approach to Old Rauma from the harbour.
