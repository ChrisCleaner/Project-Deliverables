The Chairperson drew the attention of the Committee to the two main documents of relevance to their deliberations. WHC-99/CONF.205/5 entitled &quot;Report on the state of conservation of Kakadu National Park, Australia&quot; provided a summary of information and deliberations concerning Kakadu up until the date of finalization of the document at the end of May.
