Having examined the state of conservation of the property, the Bureau considered that the primary action for the site is the establishment of the necessary regulations together with the creation of an on-site management office, to avoid further inappropriate interventions within the site.&nbsp; The Bureau recommended to the Syrian authorities to collaborate with the Secretariat in completing the draft management plan, including the proposal for the regulations.&nbsp; It also recommended that in continuing its collaboration, the Syrian authorities increase their financial contribution for the co-operation programme.
