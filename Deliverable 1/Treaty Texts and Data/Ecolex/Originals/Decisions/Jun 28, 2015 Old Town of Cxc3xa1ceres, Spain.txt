The World Heritage Committee, \n \n  Having examined  Documents WHC-15/39.COM/8B.Add and WHC-15/39.COM/INF.8B1.Add, \n  Refers  the examination of the proposed minor modification to the boundary of the buffer zone of the  Old Town of C\xc3\xa1ceres, Spain , back to the State Party in order to allow it to:\n \n Provide further explanation of the rationale chosen for the proposed limits of the buffer zone in relation to the maintenance of the Outstanding Universal Value of the property; \n Provide information on the management arrangements in place for the proposed buffer zone; \n Provide a timetable on the preparation of the Management Plan of the Old Town of C\xc3\xa1ceres and finalize it.
