The  First Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. I/5  to establish an open-ended working group to:     Review the report of the four panels referred in Dec. I/3, and integrate them into one synthesis report;   Based on (a) above, and taking into account the views expressed at the First Meeting of the Parties to the Montreal Protocol, prepare draft proposals for any amendments to the Protocol which would be needed. Such proposals are to be circulated to the Parties in accordance with article 9 of the Vienna Convention for the Protection of the Ozone Layer;   To develop the workplans referred to in Dec. I/4; and   To work out the modalities required by Dec. I/13.
