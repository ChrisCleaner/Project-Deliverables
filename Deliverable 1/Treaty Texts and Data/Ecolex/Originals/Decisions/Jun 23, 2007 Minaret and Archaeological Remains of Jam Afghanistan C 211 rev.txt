xc2\xa0  The World Heritage Committee,  1.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Having examined  Document WHC-07/31.COM/7A,  2.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Recalling  its Decision  30 COM 7A.22 , adopted at its 30th session (Vilnius, 2006),  3.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Notes  the efforts made by the State Party and the international community for the safeguarding of this property and the important progress achieved in the consolidation of the Minaret of Jam in 2006;  4.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Reiterates  its strong encouragement to the State Party to construct a footbridge and a ford across the Hari River, in order to facilitate villagers\' access from the Bedam Valley to the Jam Valley, as well as allowing a limited number of vehicles to cross the river, as set out in the recommendations of the UNESCO mission in February 2004;  5.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Adopts  the following as the desired state of conservation for the property in view of its future removal from the List of World Heritage in Danger:  a)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Increased capacity of the staff of the Afghan Ministry of Culture and Information in charge of the preservation of the property ensured;  b)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Precisely identified World Heritage property and clearly marked boundaries and buffer zones;   c)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Long-term stability and conservation of the Minaret of Jam ensured;   d)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Site security ensured;  e)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 A comprehensive management system including a long-term conservation policy developed and implemented.  6.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Encourages  the State Party to implement the following corrective measures for:  a)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Development of adequate capacity of the staff of the Afghan Ministry of Culture and Information in charge of the preservation of the property by developing and implementing an adequate training programme in conservation and management;  b)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Precise identification of the World Heritage property and clearly marked boundaries and buffer zones by:     (i)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Undertaking topographic and archaeological surface surveys and re-defining core and buffer zones, as well as identifying zones affected by illicit excavations;  (ii)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Marking of the core zone as "World Heritage protected area";  (iii)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Officially revising the boundaries of the World Heritage property according to the results of the relevant surveys in order to complement the already identified Outstanding Universal Value.  c)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Long-term consolidation and conservation of the Minaret of Jam and the archaeological remains by:  (i)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Completing the documentation and recording of the Minaret and the archaeological remains;  (ii)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Undertaking soil investigation in the vicinity of the monument in order to obtain information on the cause of the inclination of the Minaret and to define the long-term consolidation measures;  (iii)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Regular and systematic monitoring of the Minaret\'s inclination;  (iv)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Establishing a full inventory of decoration including digitalisation and reference system for all eight sides of the base of the Minaret;  (v)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Implementing emergency restoration of the surface decoration of the Minaret.  (d)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Ensured site security by:  (i)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Exerting strict control of illicit excavations and protecting the site against looting, notably through hiring of adequate number of trained site guards;  (ii)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Implementing measures enforcing the 2004 Preservation\xc2\xa0Law for Cultural and Historical Monuments ensured.  (e)\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Development and implementation of management system by undertaking appropriate training for the staff of the Ministry of Information and Culture in charge of the property;  7.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Invites  the international community, in co-operation with the World Heritage Centre, to continue its technical and financial support, in particular to achieve the above indicated desired state of conservation;  8.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Requests  the State Party, in consultation with the World Heritage Centre and ICOMOS, to develop a draft Statement of Outstanding Universal Value including the conditions of integrity and authenticity, for examination by the Committee at its 32nd session in 2008;  9.\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Also requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre by  1 February 2008  a progress report on the implementation of corrective measures, for examination by the Committee at its 32nd session in 2008; and  10. \xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0  Decides  to retain the   Minaret and Archaeological Remains of Jam (Afghanistan)   on the List of World Heritage in Danger.
