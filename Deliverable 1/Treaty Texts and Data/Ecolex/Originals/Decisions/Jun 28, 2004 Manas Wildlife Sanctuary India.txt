The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Congratulates  the State Party and welcomes its commitment to co-operate with national and international partners to raise financing for World Heritage conservation in India; \n 2.  Commends  the United Nations Foundation (UNF) and its partners for committing financial support for World Heritage conservation in India including support to Manas Wildlife Sanctuary; \n 3.  Recommends  the World Heritage Centre and IUCN to fully co-operate with the State Party, UNF and other national and international partners to begin activities benefiting Manas Wildlife Sanctuary as soon as possible; \n 4.  Requests  the World Heritage Centre and IUCN in co-operation with the State Party to undertake a mission to the property to assess the security situation and ecological conditions and submit recommendations for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 29th session in 2005; \n  5.  Decides  to retain Manas Wildlife Sanctuary on the List of World Heritage in Danger.
