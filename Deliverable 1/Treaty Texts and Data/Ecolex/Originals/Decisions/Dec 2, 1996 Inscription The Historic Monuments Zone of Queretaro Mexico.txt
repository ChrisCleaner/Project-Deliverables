The Historic Monuments Zone of Queretaro  \n  792  \n  Mexico    \n  C(ii) (iv)  \n The Committee decided to inscribe the nominated property on the basis of cultural criteria (ii) and (iv) considering that the site is of outstanding universal value and an exceptional example of a colonial town whose layout symbolizes its multi-ethnic population.
