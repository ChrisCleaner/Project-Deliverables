The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document  WHC-06/30.COM/11B , \n 2.  Recalling Decisions  29 COM 11 A.4  and  29 COM 11 A.5  adopted at its 29th session (Durban, 2005), \n 3.  Noting  proposals for changes to the nomination dossiers for some World Heritage properties in North America, \n 4.  Approves  the Statements of significance for the World Heritage properties in North America as included in Annex I of Document  WHC-06/30.COM/11B ; \n 5.  Notes  the changes to the names as indicated in Document  WHC-06/30.COM/8B , and  further notes  the adjustments to natural heritage criteria concerning geological values, as indicated in Document  WHC-06/30.COM/8D  and decides to also change the name of  Redwood National Park  to  Redwood National and State Parks ; \n 6.  Encourages  the State Party of Canada to put forward extensions to Canadian Rocky Mountain Parks and Wood Buffalo National Park, pursuant to Canada\'s Tentative List for World Heritage Sites (2004); \n 7.  Encourages  Canada and the United States of America to submit any outstanding documentation related to World Heritage properties, as soon as possible; \n 8.  Recommends  that Canada and the United States of America continue, in cooperation with other Committee members, States Parties, the Advisory Bodies and the World Heritage Centre to explore, as appropriate, the potential for developing guidelines for management plans and principles for evaluating visual impacts for activities in and adjacent to World Heritage properties; \n 9.  Encourages  Canada and the United States of America to continue their strong collaboration and to consider how to enhance collaboration with the State Party of Mexico in matters of shared interest for natural and cultural heritage. \n &nbsp;
