The Conference of the Parties        1.  appreciates    the concrete contribution of the Central European Initiative (CEI) to the Carpathian Convention and  welcomes  the scientific support provided by EURAC;     2.  takes note of    the information submitted in Annex to the Secretariat note on Sustainable transport \xe2\x80\x93Article 8 of the Carpathian Convention UNEP/CC/COP1/10;     3.  decides    to support the establishment of a Working Group on sustainable industry, energy, transport and infrastructure under the Carpathian Convention Implementation Committee;     4.  requests    the interim Secretariat to convene the first meeting of the aforementioned Working Group in order to define its terms of reference and initiate its concrete work.
