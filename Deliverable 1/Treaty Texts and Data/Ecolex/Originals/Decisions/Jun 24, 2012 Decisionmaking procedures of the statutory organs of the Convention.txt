The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Having examined  Document WHC-12/36.COM/12B, \n 2.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Recalling  Decision  35 COM 12B,  adopted at its 35th session (UNESCO, 2011), \n 3.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Welcomes  the progress made by the World Heritage Centre and the Advisory Bodies in improving the conduct of statutory meetings, the capacity building related activities, the transparency of documents and statutory meetings, as well as the clarifications provided on the various World Heritage processes; \n 4.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Requests  the World Heritage Centre and the Advisory Bodies to continue their work on the implementation of the remaining activities; \n 5.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Takes note  of the Study on oversight and monitoring mechanisms on priority policy issues prepared by the World Heritage Centre and the UNESCO Internal Oversight Service (IOS) as presented in Annex 2 of Document WHC-12/36.COM/12B; \n 6.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Decides  to assess the feasibility of an ordinary session in October/November 2013, as requested in its Decision  35 COM 12B  paragraph 9c, at its 37th session (June/July 2013); \n 7.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Also requests  the World Heritage Centre, in collaboration with the Advisory Bodies, to present the draft &ldquo;Policy Guidelines&rdquo; document for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 37th session in 2013.&nbsp;
