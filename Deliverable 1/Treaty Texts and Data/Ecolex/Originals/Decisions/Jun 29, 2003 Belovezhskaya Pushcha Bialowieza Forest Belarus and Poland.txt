The World Heritage Committee,  1.  Notes  the information provided by both States Parties;  2.  Encourages  UNESCO and IUCN to undertake a monitoring mission to visit the property in 2003 in co-operation with the States Parties to review the state of conservation of the property and possibilities for transboundary management co-operation and to meet with all relevant stakeholders in both Belarus and Poland;  3.
