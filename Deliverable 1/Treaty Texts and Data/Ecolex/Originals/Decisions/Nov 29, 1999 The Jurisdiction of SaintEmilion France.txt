N&deg;:  932  \n State Party:  France    \n Criteria:  C (iii) (iv)  \n The Committee inscribed the site on the World Heritage List on the basis of criteria ( iii)  and  (iv) : \n   Criterion (iii):   The Ancient Jurisdiction of Saint-Emilion is an outstanding example of an historic vineyard landscape that has survived intact and in activity to the present day. \n   Criterion (iv)   :  The intensive cultivation of grapes for wine production in a precisely defined region and the resulting landscape is illustrated in an exceptional way by the historic Jurisdiction of Saint-Emilion. \n The Committee expressed its appreciation for this nomination as it represents the cultural landscape typology introduced in 1992, in which the natural environment had been transformed to a landscape of monumental value.
