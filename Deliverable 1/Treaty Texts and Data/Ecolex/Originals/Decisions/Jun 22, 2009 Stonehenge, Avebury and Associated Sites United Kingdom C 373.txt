The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document WHC-09/33.COM/7B, \n 2.  Recalling  Decision  32 COM 7B.114 , adopted at its 32nd session (Quebec City, 2008), \n 3.  Regrets  that the State Party continues to make little progress in the urgent resolution of the significant A344 road closures and visitor facility issues at the property, despite assurances made as long ago as 1986; \n 4.  Requests  that the State Party keeps the World Heritage Centre informed of any progress, particularly the Ministerial announcement, as it occurs; \n 5.  Also requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 February 2011  a report on progress made on the road closure and visitor facilities, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 35th session in 2011.
