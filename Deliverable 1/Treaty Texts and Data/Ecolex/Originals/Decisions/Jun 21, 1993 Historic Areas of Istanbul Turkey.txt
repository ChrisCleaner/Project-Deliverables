The Office of the Director-General for Historic Monuments of Turkey will collect all the technical documentation on Saint Sophie on the basis of a synthesis elaborated by the Director-General of this service. A group of Turkish and international experts will meet in Istanbul in September 1993 to prepare a global action plan for the safeguard of Saint Sophie. \n A preliminary report will be presented to the Committee in December 1993. Financial support from UNESCO\'s Regular Programme will be allocated for this action which will be implemented in the framework of the International Safeguard Campaign for Istanbul.
