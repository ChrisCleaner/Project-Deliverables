The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;   Having examined   Document WHC-13/37.COM/7B, \n 2.&nbsp;   Recalling   Decision   36 COM 7B.88   , adopted at its 36th session (Saint-Petersburg, 2012), \n 3.&nbsp;   Notes   the progress with finalising and approving Special Protection Plans for sectors of the Conjunto Hist&oacute;rico, due for completion in 2013; \n 4.&nbsp;   Also notes   that the buffer zone will be completely covered by these Plans which should provide it with adequate protection; \n 5.&nbsp;   Further notes   that for the wider setting, the local authorities will be tasked with establishing adequate control measures for new constructions; \n 6.&nbsp;   Considers   that impact assessments for new constructions which can potentially impact the Outstanding Universal Value should be carried out in line with the ICOMOS Guidance on Heritage Impact Assessments; \n 7.&nbsp;   Takes note   that no collaboration with ICOMOS has so far been undertaken on studies necessary to avoid further high-rise buildings that would impact adversely on the Outstanding Universal Value, but   notes furthermore   the&nbsp; request made by the State Party to ICOMOS to start this process; \n 8.&nbsp;   Requests   the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by   1&nbsp;February&nbsp;2015   , a progress report on the implementation of the above.
