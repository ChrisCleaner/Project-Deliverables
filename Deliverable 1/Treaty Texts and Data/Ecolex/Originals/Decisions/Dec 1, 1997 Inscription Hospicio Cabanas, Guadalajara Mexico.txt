Hospicio Cabanas, Guadalajara   \n  815   \n  Mexico     \n  C(i)(ii)(iii)(iv)   \n The Committee decided to inscribe this property on the basis of criteria (i), (ii), (iii) and (iv), considering that the Hospicio Caba&ntilde;as is a unique architectural complex, designed to respond to social and economic requirements for housing the sick, the aged, the young, and the needy, which provides an outstanding solution of great subtlety and humanity.
