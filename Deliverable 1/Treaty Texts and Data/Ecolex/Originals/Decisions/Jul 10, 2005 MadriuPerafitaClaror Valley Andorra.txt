The World Heritage Committee,  1.  Having  examined Document   WHC-05/29.COM/7B.Rev  ,  2.  Recalling  its Decision  28 COM 15B.36  adopted at its 28th session (Suzhou, 2004),  3.  Thanks  the State Party of Andorra for having submitted a comprehensive update as requested;  4.  Notes  that the legal protection has been addressed through a decree which ensures the protection of both natural and cultural values of the Valley, and that the decree is to be adopted in June 2005;  5.  Requests  the State Party to inform the World Heritage Centre about the implementation of the management plan and on the adoption of the decree on the legal protection of the property and its implementation, by  1 February 2006  at the latest.
