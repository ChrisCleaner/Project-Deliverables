The  Fifth Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. V/17:      To note with appreciation the work by the Technology and Economic Assessment Panel regarding the feasibility of banning or restricting the import of products produced with, but not containing, controlled substances;   That it is not feasible to impose a ban or restriction on the import of such products under the Protocol at this stage;   To request the Technology and Economic Assessment Panel to review this issue at regular intervals.
