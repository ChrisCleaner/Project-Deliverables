The World Heritage Committee, \n \n  Having examined  Document WHC-15/39.COM/7B.Add, \n  Requests  the State Party to undertake an assessment of current and potential impacts on the Outstanding Universal Value (OUV) and integrity of the property of already existing and planned infrastructure of the Las Pailas geothermal project and its expansion located outside and adjacent to the property in line with IUCN\xe2\x80\x99s World Heritage Advice Note on Environmental Assessment, and  urges  the State Party to finalize such an assessment by 1 February 2016 and submit it to the World Heritage Centre for review by IUCN, in accordance with Paragraph 172 of the  Operational Guidelines ; \n  Encourages  the State Party to invite an advisory mission, to be financed by the State Party, aiming to assess the situation, in light with the outcomes of the requested report, as in paragraph 2; \n  Also requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 February 2016  , a progress report  and by  1 December 2016 , an updated report, including a 1-page executive summary, on the state of conservation of the property for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 41st session in 2017.
