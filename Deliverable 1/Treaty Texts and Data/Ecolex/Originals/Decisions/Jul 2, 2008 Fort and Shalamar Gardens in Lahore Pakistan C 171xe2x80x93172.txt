The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document  WHC-08/32.COM/7A , \n 2.  Recalling  Decision  31 COM 7A.24 , adopted at its 31st session (Christchurch, 2007), \n 3.  Notes  the progress made by the State Party in the overall preservation and conservation of Lahore Fort and Shalamar  Gardens; \n 4.  Reiterates  its requests to the State Party to develop, in consultation with the World Heritage Centre and the Advisory Bodies, a draft Statement of Outstanding Universal Value including the conditions of integrity and authenticity, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 33rd session in 2009; \n 5.  Requests  the State Party to invite a joint World Heritage Centre / ICOMOS Reactive Monitoring mission so as to assess the progress made towards the Desired state of conservation for the removal of the property from the List of World Heritage in Danger, for its examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 33rd session in 2009; \n 6.  Recommends  that the State Party submit to the World Heritage Committee a formal request for the modification of the boundaries of the property; \n 7.  Also requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre by  1 February 2009,  a progress report, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 33rd session in 2009; \n 8.   Decides  to retain the Fort and Shalamar Gardens in Lahore (Pakistan) on the List of World Heritage in Danger.
