The World Heritage Committee,  1.  Decides  not to inscribe  Parque Nacional del Este and its buffer zone ,  Dominican Republic , on the World Heritage List on the basis of natural criteria;  2.  Decides  to refer back to the State Party the nomination of  Parque Nacional del Este and its buffer zone ,  Dominican Republic , encouraging the State Party to undertake the survey programme recommended by ICOMOS and to provide assurances that the resources, management, facilities, and security issues noted by ICOMOS will be examined and responded to.
