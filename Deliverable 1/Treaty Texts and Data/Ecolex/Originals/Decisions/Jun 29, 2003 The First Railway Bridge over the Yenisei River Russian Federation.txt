With regard to  T  he First Railway Bridge over the Yenisei River ,  Russian Federation , the World Heritage Committee,  1.  Considers  that the conditions required by paragraph 67 of the  Operational Guidelines  concerning &quot;damage from disaster caused by natural events or human activities&quot; have not been fully met;   2.  Decides  not to inscribe  The First Railway Bridge over the Yenisei River ,  Russian Federation , on the World Heritage List on an emergency basis;   3.  Encourages  the State Party to resubmit the nomination in accordance with normal procedures for nomination.
