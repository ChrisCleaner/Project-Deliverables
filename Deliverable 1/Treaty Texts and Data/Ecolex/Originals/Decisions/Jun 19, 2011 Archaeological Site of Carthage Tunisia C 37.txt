The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document WHC-11/35.COM/7B, \n 2.  Takes   note  of the report submitted by the State Party and the information provided on the state of conservation of the property; \n 3.  Welcomes  the cancellation of the decrees of declassification within the Archaeological Park of Carthage-Sidi Bou Said; \n 4.  Encourages  the State Party to submit a minor boundary modification in order to define a sufficient buffer zone to preserve the integrity of the property, and to indicate the legal framework of protection; \n 5.  Urges  the State Party to complete, adopt and implement the Plan for the Protection and Enhancement of the property initiated since 1996; \n 6.  Requests  the State Party to invite a joint World Heritage Centre / ICOMOS reactive monitoring mission to assess the state of conservation of the property and the progress regarding the Plan for its protection and enhancement; \n 7.  Also requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1   February 2012 , an updated report on the state of conservation of the property and the implementation of the above recommendations for consideration by the World Heritage Committee at its 36th session in 2012.
