The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.\xc2\xa0   Having examined   Documents WHC-13/37.COM/8B, WHC-13/37.COM/INF.8B1and WHC-13/37.COM/INF.8B4, \n 2.\xc2\xa0   Recognizing   the Outstanding Universal Value of the site,   refers   the nomination of the   Cultural Landscape of Maymand ,   Iran     (Islamic Republic of)   back to the State Party, in order to allow it to set the property into its wider agro-pastoral context, and demonstrate in which way the site is an outstanding reflection of transhumance in its geo-cultural region; \n 3.\xc2\xa0   Requests   the State Party and the Advisory Bodies to continue to work closely on the nomination dossier as well as with the other States Parties, especially those in the region, to promote the concept of Desert Cultural Landscape; \n 4.\xc2\xa0   Also requests   the State Party to develop a land-use strategy that integrates traditional agro-pastoralism into an economic development strategy.
