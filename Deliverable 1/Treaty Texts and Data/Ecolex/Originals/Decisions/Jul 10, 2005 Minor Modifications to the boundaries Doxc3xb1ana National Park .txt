The World Heritage Committee,  1.  Having examined  Documents   WHC-05/29.COM/8B  ,   WHC-05/29.COM/8B.Add 2   and   WHC-05/29.COM/INF.8B.2  ,  2.  Decides  to extend  Do\xc3\xb1ana National Park  (Spain) to bring the boundaries of the World Heritage property in line with the extended National Park; thus the total area of the World Heritage property will be 54,251.7 ha;  3.  Commends  the State Party of Spain for its efforts to enhance the protection and management of the property.
