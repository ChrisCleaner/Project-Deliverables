The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document  WHC-09/33.COM/7A.Add , \n  2.   Recalling  Decision  32 COM 7A.17  adopted at its 32nd session (Quebec City, 2008), \n  3.   Requests  the State Party, should the situation allow it, to implement the corrective measures identified: \n a) Establishment of a local management unit on the site, \n b) Preparation and implementation of a conservation and management plan, \n c) Maintenance and emergency conservation activities; \n  4.   Reiterates its request  to the State Party to develop, in consultation with the World Heritage Centre and the Advisory Bodies, a draft Statement of Outstanding Universal Value and a proposal for the desired state of conservation for the removal of the property from the List of World Heritage in Danger, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 34th session in 2010; \n  5.   Encourages  the State Party to submit an International Assistance request for technical cooperation in order to address the damages to the property, and the need to develop remedial strategies which take into account the geographical extent of the property and its present security conditions; \n  6.   Calls upon  the international community to provide all necessary assistance to the State Party in the protection of this property; \n  7.   Also   requests  the State Party, should the conditions allow it, to invite a joint World Heritage Centre/ICOMOS reactive monitoring mission to Samarra to assess the state of conservation of the property; \n  8.   Further requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 February 2010 , a report for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 34th session in 2010; \n  9.  Decides  to retain Samarra Archaeological City (Iraq) on the List of World Heritage in Danger.
