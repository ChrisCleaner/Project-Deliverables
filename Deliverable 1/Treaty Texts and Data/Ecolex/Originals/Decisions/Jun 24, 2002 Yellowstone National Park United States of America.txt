The World Heritage Committee,  1.  Notes  that the State Party has prepared the draft Supplementary Environmental Impact Study and has received 350,000 comments which are currently being evaluated;   2.  Urges  the State Party to continue to report on Yellowstone\'s snowmobile phase-out and other efforts to ensure that winter travel facilities respect the protection of the Park, its visitors, and its wildlife;  3.  Invites  the State Party to co-operate with IUCN and the Centre to prepare a report for submission to its 27th session in June/July 2003, containing steps it intends to take to develop action plans and define parameters and conditions to monitor progress in the restoration of the integrity of the site with a view to facilitating the Committee\'s future considerations for removing the site from the List of World Heritage in Danger; 4.  Decides  to retain the site in the List of World Heritage in Danger.
