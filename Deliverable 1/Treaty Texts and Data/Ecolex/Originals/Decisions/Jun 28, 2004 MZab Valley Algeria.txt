The World Heritage Committee,\n1. Requests the State Party to proceed with the listing of the M\'Zab Valley as a\nsafeguarded sector and to elaborate a Plan for the Safeguarding and\nPresentation of this sector;\n2. Strongly urges the State Party to restore the hydraulic network to preserve the\nhistoric value of the water management system with a view to sustainable\ndevelopment.\n3. Calls upon the State Party to undertake appropriate measures, notably with\nregard to the designation of non-constructible areas, for the protection of the\nurban and landscape qualities of the property.\n4. Requests the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Committee, by 1\nFebruary 2006, a report on the implementation of the above-mentioned\nactivities, for examination by the Committee at its 30th session in 2006.
