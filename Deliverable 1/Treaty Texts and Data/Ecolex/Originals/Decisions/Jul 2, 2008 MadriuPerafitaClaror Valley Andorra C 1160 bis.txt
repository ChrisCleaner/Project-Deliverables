The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document  WHC-08/32.COM/7B.Add , \n 2.  Recalling  Decision  30 COM 7B.80 , adopted at its 30th session (Vilnius, 2006), \n 3.  Notes  that the Law to protect the property as a cultural landscape entered into force in July 2006; \n 4.  Also notes  delays in finalizing the management plan while awaiting the outcome of legal challenges; \n 5.  Requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 February 2010 , a progress report on the implementation of the management plan, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 34th session in 2010.
