The  Second Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. II/17  with regard to the budget to adopt the system of rolling biennial budgets, and to approve a total revised budget of $3,400,000 for 1990, a total revised budget for 1991 of $2,423,000 and a total budget for 1992 of $2,225,000. The details of the approved budgets are presented in Annex VI to the report on the work of the Second Meeting of the Parties.
