The World Heritage Committee,\n1. Noting the outcome of the joint UNESCO-ICOMOS mission to the property\nand that the requested Environmental Impact Assessment for the proposed\nwaste incinerator has been provided to the World Heritage Centre and the\nAdvisory Body, ICOMOS,\n2. Urges the State Party to consider all recommendations made by the UNESCOICOMOS\nmonitoring mission of February 2004, in particular concerning\nvisual and polluting impacts as well as buffer zone definition;\n3. Requests that the World Heritage Centre be kept informed of any further\nchanges in the design of the incinerator as well as the completion of the project\nin order to confirm that the visual impacts are as minor as anticipated.
