The  Seventh Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. VII/14:      To note that the implementation of the Protocol by those Parties that have reported data is satisfactory;   To note with regret that only 82 Parties out of 126 that should have reported data for 1993 have reported and that only 60 Parties have reported data for 1994;   To note that the timely reporting of data and any other required information is a legal obligation for each Party and to request all Parties to comply with the provisions of Articles 7 and 9 of the Protocol.
