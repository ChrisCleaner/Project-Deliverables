The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;  Having examined  Documents WHC-12/36.COM/8B.Add and WHC-12/36.COM/INF.8B1.Add, \n 2.&nbsp;  Decides not to approve  the proposed reduction of the buffer zone for the  Fortress of Suomenlinna, Finland ; \n 3.&nbsp;  Requests  the State Party to put in place for the existing buffer zone zoning that reflects different planning needs, and which could be recognised by land-use and development plans.
