The World Heritage Committee,      Having examined  Document WHC/16/40.COM/7A.Add,    Recalling  Decisions\xc2\xa0 37 COM 7B.57 ,  38 COM 7A.12  and  39 COM 7A.36 , adopted at its 37th (Phnom Penh, 2013), 38th (Doha, 2014) and 39th (Bonn, 2015) sessions respectively,    Taking into account  Decision  40 COM 7A.22  on the World Heritage properties of the Syrian Arab Republic, adopted at its 40th session (Istanbul, 2016),    Acknowledges  the efforts of the local communities to raise awareness of the need to protect the property despite the very difficult circumstances;    Deplores  the breaking of the temporary ceasefire agreement within the property in December 2015 which resulted in further severe damages and illegal excavations;    Urges  all parties to pursue all possible cooperation for ensuring the respect of a ceasefire within the property;     Decides    to retain the Ancient City of Bosra (Syrian Arab Republic) on the List of World Heritage in Danger
