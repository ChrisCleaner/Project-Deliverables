The World Heritage Committee, \n \n  Having examined  Documents WHC-15/39.COM/8B and WHC-15/39.COM/INF.8B1 ,  \n  Defers  the examination of the nomination of  The Naumburg Cathedral and the Landscape of the Rivers Saale and Unstrut \xe2\x80\x93 Territories of Power in the High Middle Ages, Germany , to the World Heritage List in order to allow the State Party to:\n \n further explore the relationship between the Naumburg Cathedral and the surrounding landscape; \n strengthen the representativeness of the territorial and urban organization for the period of the High Middle Ages and the significance of the related monuments; \n redefine the boundaries of the site taking into account the concerns put forward by ICOMOS so as to ensure adequate protection; \n  submit  \xe2\x80\x93 on the basis of the above-mentioned recommendations \xe2\x80\x93 a significantly revised nomination, which will require an expert mission to the site; \n \n \n  Suggests  that the State Party consider inviting ICOMOS to offer advice and guidance.
