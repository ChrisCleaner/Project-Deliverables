The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Documents  WHC-09/33.COM/8B  and  WHC-09/33.COM/INF.8B1.Add , \n 2.  Refers  the examination of the proposed buffer zones for  Tipasa ,  Algeria , back to the State Party to allow it to: \n a) Revise the boundary of the proposed buffer zone to include the port area; \n b) Provide details of the status of the buffer zone and what protection it will afford the inscribed property as well as what protection will be given to archaeological remains associated with those in the property; \n c) Clarify whether the Kbor er Roumia will be provided with a buffer zone.
