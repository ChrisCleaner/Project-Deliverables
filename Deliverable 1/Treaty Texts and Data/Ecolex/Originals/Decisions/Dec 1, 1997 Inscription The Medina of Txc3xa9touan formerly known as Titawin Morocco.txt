The Medina of T&eacute;touan (formerly known as Titawin)  \n  837   \n  Morocco   \n  C(ii)(iv)(v)  \n The Committee decided to inscribe the Medina of T&eacute;touan (formerly known as Titawin) on the basis of criteria (ii), (iv) and (v), considering that it is an exceptionally well preserved and complete example of this type of historic town, displaying all the features of the high Andalusian culture. \n &nbsp;
