A.2 TRAINING  \n  A.2.2 International Study Project for the Conservation of Wadi Tumilat (Egypt) (US$ 26,000 requested)  \n The Bureau  approved  an amount of  US$ 20,000  for the international training project for a scientific study and the conservation of the region of Wadi Tumilat. Organized by the Universities of Cairo, London, and Uppsala, the project foresees the participation of 20 students in documentation and conservation research work.
