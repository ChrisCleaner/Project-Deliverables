The Conference of the Parties   1.  Having considered  the request by the European Commission \xe2\x80\x93 DG Environment to consult with the Parties and Observers of the Carpathian Convention a possible proposal for the establishment of a Working Group on Adaptation to Climate Change under the Carpathian Convention supported by the project CARPIVIA (Carpathian integrated assessment of vulnerability to climate change and ecosystem-based adaptation measures, www.carpivia.eu) that is funded by the European Commission under the above mentioned preparatory action;  2.  Emphasizing  the importance of climate change and its impact for the implementation of the Carpathian Convention;  3.  Decides  to establish a Working Group on Adaptation to Climate Change and adopts its Terms of Reference (UNEP/CC/COP3/DOC12).
