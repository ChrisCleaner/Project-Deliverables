The  Thirteenth Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. XIII/18:      To note that Armenia is in non-compliance with data reporting requirement under Article 7 of the Protocol, based on which compliance with the phase-out schedule is determined;   To note that ratification of the London Amendment is required to qualify for financial assistance from international funding agencies;   To recommend that, should Armenia ratify the London Amendment to the Montreal Protocol, international funding agencies should consider favourably the provision of financial assistance to Armenia for projects to phase out ozone-depleting substances in that country.
