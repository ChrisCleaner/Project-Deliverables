The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;   Having examined   Documents WHC-13/37.COM/8B.Add and WHC-13/37.COM/INF.8B1.Add, \n 2.&nbsp;   Refers   the examination of the proposed buffer zone for   Burgos Cathedral   ,   Spain   , back to the State Party in order to allow it to: \n a)&nbsp; Provide a detailed overview of the site management arrangements that would be put in place in the proposed buffer zone; and in relation to both World Heritage properties; \n b)&nbsp; Provide a map showing the relationship between the two World Heritage properties of Burgos Cathedral and the Route of Santiago de Compostela within Burgos.
