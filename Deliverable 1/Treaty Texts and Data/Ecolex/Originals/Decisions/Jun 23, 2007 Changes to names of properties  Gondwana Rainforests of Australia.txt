The World Heritage Committee,    Having examined  Document WHC-07/31.COM/8B ,    Approves  the proposed name change to the Central Eastern Rainforest Reserves (Australia) as proposed by the Australian authorities. The name of the property becomes  Gondwana Rainforests of Australia  in English and  Les for\xc3\xaats humides Gondwana de l\'Australie  in French.
