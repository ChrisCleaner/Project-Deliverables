The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Having examined  Documents WHC-12/36.COM/8B.Add and WHC-12/36.COM/INF.8B1.Add, \n 2.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Refers  the examination of the proposed minor modification to the boundary of  Historic Centre of Santa Cruz de Mompox, Colombia  back to the State Party in order to allow it to: \n a)&nbsp;&nbsp; Extend the property boundary only to the east to include the river and east bank to the extent of the original width of the river opposite the currently inscribed property and for the length of the currently inscribed property, \n b)&nbsp;&nbsp; Provide an adequate map of the property and buffer zone indicating their precise boundaries either topographic or cadastral, presented at a scale which is appropriate to the size in hectares of the property, including title and legend in English and bearing a labelled coordinate grid; \n 3.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Approves  the proposed buffer zone for  Historic Centre of Santa Cruz de Mompox, Colombia .
