A.2 TRAINING  \n  A.2.3 Training Workshop for Urban Planning Officers of China\'s Historic Cities (US$ 25,000 requested)  \n The Bureau, \n  recognizing  the urgent need to sensitize the municipal authori ties and increase their technical competence to safeguard the historic cities of China, \n  approved  an amount of  US$ 25,000  to organize a workshop in May 1997 for the preparation of the Conference for the Mayors of Historic Cities, foreseen in September 1997.
