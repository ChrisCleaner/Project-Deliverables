The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;   Having examined   Document WHC-13/37.COM/7A.Add, \n 2.&nbsp;   Recalling   Decision   35 COM 7C   , adopted at its 35th&nbsp;session (UNESCO, 2011), \n 3.&nbsp;   Welcomes   the publication of the Guidance on the Desired State of conservation for the removal of a property from the List of World Heritage Danger (DSOCR); \n 4.&nbsp;   Requests   the World Heritage Centre and Advisory Bodies to continue supporting States Parties in developing and submitting DSOCRs for all properties included in the List of World Heritage in Danger, by its 40th session in 2016 at the latest, and   considers   that properties should be retained on the List of World Heritage in Danger until the Desired state of conservation for removal is met.
