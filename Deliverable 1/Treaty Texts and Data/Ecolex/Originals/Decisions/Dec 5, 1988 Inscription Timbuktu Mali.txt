Timbuktu  \n  119 rev  \n  Mali  \n  C(ii)(iv)(v)  \n The Committee decided to inscribe the three large mosques and sixteen cemeteries and mausoleums of Timbuktu. In view of the threat of sand encroachment at Timbuktu, the Committee suggests that the Mali authorities consider inscription of this property on the List of World Heritage in Danger. \n &nbsp;
