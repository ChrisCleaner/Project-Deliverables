The  First Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. I/2  to establish its Bureau to be composed of the President, three Vice-Presidents and Rapporteur elected by each meeting of the Parties.   The Bureau shall meet at least once between meetings of the Parties to review the work of any working groups established by the Parties during their meetings, to consider other topics on the Agenda of the next meeting of the Parties and to review the documents prepared by the Secretariat for meetings of the Parties to facilitate the work of these meetings.
