The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;   Having examined   Document WHC-13/37.COM/18B, \n 2.&nbsp;   Recalling   Decision   36 COM 12B   adopted at its 36th&nbsp;session; \n 3.&nbsp;   Considering   the costs inherent to the holding of an additional ordinary session of the World Heritage Committee and the financial situation that UNESCO, including its World Heritage Centre, is facing; \n 4.&nbsp;   Decides   not to hold an additional ordinary session in October/November 2013; \n 5.&nbsp;   Requests   the World Heritage Centre to re-assess the eventual possibility of holding an additional ordinary session of this Committee in 2015 and to present a report on this issue at its 39th session in 2015.&nbsp;
