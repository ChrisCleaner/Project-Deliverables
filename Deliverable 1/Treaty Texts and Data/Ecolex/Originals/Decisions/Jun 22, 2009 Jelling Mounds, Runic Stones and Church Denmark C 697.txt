The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document WHC-09/33.COM/7B, \n 2.  Takes note  of the results of the ICOMOS advisory mission to the property; \n 3.  Requests  the State Party to: \n a) Urgently provide information on any conservation and development plan to address the issue of an environmental covering for the stones to the World Heritage Centre for review by ICOMOS, \n b) Consider extending the buffer zone in consultation with the World Heritage Centre and ICOMOS; \n 4.  Also requests  the State Party to submit a report on the steps taken to implement the advisory mission recommendations to the World Heritage Centre by  1 February 2011  for review by ICOMOS.
