The  Ninth Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. IX/11:      To note that the implementation of the Protocol by those Parties that have reported data is satisfactory;   To note with regret that only 113 Parties out of 152 that should have reported data for 1995 have reported to date and that only 43 Parties have to date reported data for 1996;   To remind all Parties to comply with the provisions of Articles 7 and 9 of the Protocol.
