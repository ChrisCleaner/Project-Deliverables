The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document WHC-11/35.COM/7B.Add, \n 2.  Recalling  Decision  34 COM 7B.59 , adopted at its 34th session (Brasilia, 2010), \n 3.  Encourages  the State Party when conditions permit to implement the recommendations contained in the mission report, and in particular to consider a stakeholder workshop to be organized with the World Heritage Centre and the Advisory Bodies to address the many dimensions of an appropriate conservation strategy for the property; \n 4.  Requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1&nbsp;February&nbsp;2012,  a detailed report on the above-mentioned issues, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 36th session in 2012.
