The World Heritage Committee, \n 1. &nbsp; Having examined  Document  WHC-08/32.COM/8B , \n 2. &nbsp; Approves  the proposed name change to Jungfrau-Aletsch-Bietschhorn as proposed by the Swiss authorities. The name of the property becomes  Swiss Alps Jungfrau-Aletsch  in English and  Alpes suisses Jungfrau-Aletsch  in French.
