The General Assembly,       Having examined  Document WHC-13/19.GA/10,    Recalling  Resolutions  17 GA 9  and  18 GA 11  adopted at the 17th (UNESCO, 2009) and 18th sessions (UNESCO, 2011) of the General Assembly of States Parties respectively, and the adoption of the Strategic Action Plan and Vision by the 18th General Assembly of States Parties (UNESCO, 2011);    Welcomes  the progress made in the Implementation Plan of the Strategic Action Plan in particular with regards to links between the Draft Implementation Plan and the follow-up to Resolution  18 GA 8  of the General Assembly of States Parties (UNESCO, 2011);    Requests  the World Heritage Centre, in collaboration with the Advisory Bodies, and with the support of States Parties to continue its implementation efforts;    Also requests  that a progress report on the implementation of the Strategic Action Plan be presented to the General Assembly at its 20th session for its consideration.
