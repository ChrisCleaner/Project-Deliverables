The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document WHC-11/35.COM/7A, \n 2.  Recalling  Decision  34 COM 7B.27  adopted at its 34th session (Brasilia, 2010), \n 3.  Notes  the efforts made by the State Party in the implementation of the World Heritage Committee\'s decisions with regard to the corrective measures aimed at future removal of the property from the List of World Heritage in Danger; \n 4.  Ur  ges  the State Party to submit proposals for a buffer zone as a minor boundary modification, as well as to develop and finalize the Urban Land-Use Master Plan of the City of Mtskheta; \n 5.  Also urges  the State Party to adopt legislation that ensures adequate protection of the property and of any defined buffer zone and wider setting so as to sustain its Outstanding Universal Value; \n 6.  Encourages  the State Party to continue developing strategies to enhance awareness of World Heritage among stakeholders and developers; \n 7.  Requests  the State Party to invite a joint World Heritage Centre/ICOMOS/ICCROM reactive monitoring mission to the property to assess the progress in the implementation of the corrective measures; \n 8.  Also requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 February 2012 , a detailed state of conservation report, including a progress report on the implementation of the above, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 36thsession in2012; \n 9.   Decides    to retain the Historical Monuments of Mtskheta (Georgia) on the List of World Heritage in Danger.
