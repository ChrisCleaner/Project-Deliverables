The Defence Line of Amsterdam  \n  759  \n  Netherlands    \n  C(ii) (iv) (v)  \n The Committee decided to inscribe the nominated property on the basis of cultural criteria (ii), (iv) and (v) considering that the site is of outstanding universal value as it is an exceptional example of an extensive integrated defence system of the modern period which has survived intact and well conserved since it was created in the later 19th century. It is also notable for the unique way in which the Dutch genius for hydraulic engineering has been incorporated into the defences of the nation\'s capital city. \n &nbsp;
