The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;   Having examined   Document WHC-13/37.COM/7B, \n 2.&nbsp;   Recalling   Decision   35 COM 7B.66   adopted at its 35th&nbsp;session (UNESCO, 2011), \n 3.&nbsp;   Acknowledges   the steps taken by the State Party to address the removal of debris of the collapsed bridge and the relocation of the vehicular bridge outside of the property; \n 4.&nbsp;   Reiterates its request   to the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre the completed Integrated Management Plan together with a synthesis and a prioritisation of existing recommendations and intentions, for review by the World Heritage Centre and the Advisory Bodies, \n 5.&nbsp;   Also acknowledges   the information provided by the State Party concerning the demolition works in the Hampi bazaar area following a decision of the High Court of Karnataka and the compensation scheme for affected families; \n 6.&nbsp;   Encourages   the initiatives of the State Party to elaborate, in close cooperation with the local community, a strategy and action plan for the bazaar area to: \n a)&nbsp; Develop within the IMP necessary legal and planning tools to prevent any further encroachments at the Hampi bazaar, \n b)&nbsp; Develop a conservation strategy for the protection of the historic mandapas near the Virupaksha temple, in line with the IMP.&nbsp; \n 7. &nbsp; Requests   the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by   1&nbsp;February&nbsp;2015   , an updated report on the implementation of the above, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 39th&nbsp;session in 2015
