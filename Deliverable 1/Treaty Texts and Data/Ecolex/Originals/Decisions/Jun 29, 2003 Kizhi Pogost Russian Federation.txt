The World Heritage Committee [43],  1.  Recalling  its decisions taken at the 25th extraordinary session of the Bureau in 2001 (Helsinki),;  2.  Expresses its appreciation  to the authorities of the State Party for their commitment to the preservation of the property;  3.  Takes note of  the report and recommendations provided by the International Workshop with regard to the future conservation of this property under threat;   4.  Encourages  the State Party, the World Heritage Centre and the Advisory Bodies to continue to collaborate and to closely follow the future development of the conservation works;   5.  Requests  the State Party to provide an updated report the World Heritage Centre by 1 February 2004 on progress made in order that the World Heritage Committee can examine the state of conservation of the property at its 28th session in 2004.   [43]\xc2\xa0 Decision adopted without discussion.
