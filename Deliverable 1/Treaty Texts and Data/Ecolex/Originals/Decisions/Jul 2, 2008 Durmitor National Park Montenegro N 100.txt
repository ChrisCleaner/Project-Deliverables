The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document  WHC-08/32.COM/7B , \n 2.  Recalling  Decision  31 COM 7B.29 , adopted at its 31st session (Christchurch, 2007), \n 3.  Regrets  the fires which occurred in August 2007 in the vicinity of the property; \n 4.  Encourages  the State Party to include risk reduction in its management of the property, particularly in relation to the impact of fires on the Outstanding Universal Value of the property; \n 7.  Requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 February 2009,  a clarification on the status and exact location of the hydro-electric dams for which tenders were issued in November 2007, a report on its management of risks to the values of the property, and a report on the overall state of conservation of the property, for review by the World Heritage Centre and IUCN.
