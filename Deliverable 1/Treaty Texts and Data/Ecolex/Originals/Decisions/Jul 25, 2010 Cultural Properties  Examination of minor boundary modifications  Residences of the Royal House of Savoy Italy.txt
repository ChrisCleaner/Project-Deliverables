The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Documents WHC-10/34.COM/8B and WHC-10/34.COM/INF.8B1.Add, \n 2.  Approves  the minor modification to the boundary of  Castello di Pollenzo , component part of the  Residences of the Royal House of Savoy ,  Italy ; \n 3.  Approves  the buffer zone for  Castello di Pollenzo, Castello del Valentino, Villa della Regina, Castello di Moncalieri  and  Castello di Govone , component parts of the  Residences of the Royal House of Savoy ,  Italy ; \n 4.  Approves  the extension to the buffer zone for  Castello di Rivoli, Reggia di Venaria Reale, Castello di Agli&egrave;  and  Castello di Racconigi ,   component parts of the  Residences of the Royal House of Savoy ,  Italy ; \n 5.  Recommends  that the State Party consider, when possible, future extensions to the buffer zones of the Residences of the Royal House of Savoy, in terms of the historical connections between the Residences and the "command centre" in Turin, their axial relationships, views and vistas.
