NATURAL HERITAGE  \n   A.1&nbsp; Requests on which the Bureau took a decision   \n  A.1.2 Technical co-operation  \n  A.1.2.6 Mount Kilimanjaro (Tanzania)  \n The Bureau  examined  a technical cooperation request for  US$25,000  received by IUCN just prior to the meeting. IUCN informed the Bureau, that the management plan for the site has been completed. This project involves some limited road construction, a parking area and a new hiking trail to better serve visitors to the Park. The Bureau  approved  the sum requested. \n &nbsp;
