The World Heritage Committee, \n \n   Having examined   Document WHC-13/37.COM/5D and Document WHC-13/37 COM/INF.5D, \n   Adopts   the revised PACT strategy to improve the development of partnerships for World Heritage with the private sector, their implementation and their evaluation through adequate tools and guidelines; \n   Requests &nbsp;the Secretariat to continue to report, in an analytical manner, on the implementation of the PACT strategy by submitting at each regular session of the World Heritage Committee a detailed inventory of on-going and envisaged partnerships as an Annex to the Report of the World Heritage Centre, using the current template.
