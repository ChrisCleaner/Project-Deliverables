Jesuit Missions of La Santisima  Trinidad   de   Parana   and   Jesus de Tavarangue   \n  648&nbsp;  \n  Paraguay  \n  C(iv)   \n The Committee inscribed the site on the World Heritage List under criterion (iv). The Committee invited Brazil, Argentina and Paraguay to consider a joint inscription of the Jesuit missions on their territories. The Delegate of Brazil would welcome a joint conservation effort and announced that such an initiative was being taken in the context of MERCOSUR. \n &nbsp;
