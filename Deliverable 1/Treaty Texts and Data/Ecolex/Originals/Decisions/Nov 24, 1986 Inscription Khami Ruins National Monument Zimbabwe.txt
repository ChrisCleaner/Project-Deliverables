Khami   Ruins National Monument    \n  365  \n  Zimbabwe    \n  C(iii)(iv)  \n The Committee shared the concerns expressed by ICOMOS on the state of preservation of the site which was seriously deteriorating due to the climatic conditions and the encroaching vegetation.
