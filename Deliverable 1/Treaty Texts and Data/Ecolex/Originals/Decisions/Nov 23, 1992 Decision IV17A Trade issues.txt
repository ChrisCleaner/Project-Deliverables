The  Fourth Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. IV/17A:      to take note of the information provided by some Parties on the implementation of Article 4 of the Protocol and to encourage further those Parties that have not yet done so to provide the information to the Secretariat as soon as possible;   to clarify, as follows, the situation of Parties that have not ratified the London Amendment:     under paragraph 2 of Article 4 of the Protocol, the export ban on Annex A substances shall apply only to any State not party to the Montreal Protocol of 1987;   under paragraph 2  bis  of Article 4 of the Protocol, the export ban on Annex B substances shall commence only on 10 August 1993.
