Jiuzhaigou Valley Scenic and Historic Interest Area  \n  637  \n  China, People\'s&nbsp; Republic of   \n  N(iii)  \n The Committee inscribed this site of 72,000 ha on the World Heritage List. The Committee expressed concern over the question of growing human impact in the reserve and strongly encouraged the Chinese authorities to take appropriate measures to safeguard this site. The Committee recommended the State Party to prepare a species conservation status report in order to study the possibility that the site may also qualify for inscription under natural heritage criterion (iv). \n &nbsp;
