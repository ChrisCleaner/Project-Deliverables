The World Heritage Committee, \n \n  Having examined  Document WHC-07/31.COM/8B ,  \n  Approves  the proposed name change to I Sassi di Matera as proposed by the Italian authorities. The name of the property becomes the  The Sassi and the park of the Rupestrian Churches of Matera  in English and  Les Sassi et le parc des &eacute;glises rupestres de Matera  in French.
