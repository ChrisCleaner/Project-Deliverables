The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  Having examined  Document WHC-07/31.COM/7B, \n 2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  Recalling  Decision  30 COM 7B.60 , adopted at its 30th session (Vilnius, 2006), \n 3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  Notes with satisfaction  the efforts made by the State Party towards the rehabilitation of the property, in co-operation with UNESCO and other international partners, following the earthquake of May 2006; \n 4.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  Recommends  to the State Party to continue these efforts, in close co-ordination with the World Heritage Centre, ICOMOS and ICCROM, including by organizing periodical experts&rsquo; meetings to review the progress of the rehabilitation works and identify appropriate strategies; \n 5.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  Encourages  the international community to support the implementation of the Action Plan defined at the International Expert Meeting of March 2007; \n 6.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  Requests  the State Party to submit a report on the state of conservation of the property by  1 February 2009 , with information on the progress made in the implementation of the above-mentioned Action Plan, for the consideration of the Committee at its 33rd session in 2009.&nbsp;
