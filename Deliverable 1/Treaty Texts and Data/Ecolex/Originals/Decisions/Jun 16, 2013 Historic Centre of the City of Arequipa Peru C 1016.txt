The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;   Having examined   Document WHC-13/37.COM/7B, \n 2.&nbsp;   Recalling   Decision   36 COM 7B.104,   adopted at its 36th session (Saint-Petersburg, 2012), \n 3.&nbsp;   Takes note   of the information provided by the State Party on the actions taken to implement the recommendations made by the World Heritage Committee and   reiterates its concern   that measures to ensure the conservation and protection of the property continue to be at the planning stages; \n 4.&nbsp;   Reiterates its request   to the State Party to finalize the following documents for submission to the World Heritage Centre by   30 November 2013   , for evaluation: \n a)&nbsp; Risk Preparedness plan for the property, \n b)&nbsp; Delineation of the buffer zone and approval of adequate regulatory measures, \n c)&nbsp; Master Plan for the property in three printed copies, in addition to an electronic, for review by the Advisory Bodies, \n d)&nbsp; Proposal for a minor boundary modification, according to the procedure established by the   Operational Guidelines   ; \n 5.&nbsp;   Urges   the State Party to finalize the management plan for the property as it has been requested by the World Heritage Committee since 2009 and submit three copies to the World Heritage Centre, for review by the Advisory Bodies by   1 February 2014   ; \n 6.&nbsp;   Also reiterates its request   to the State Party to undertake an Environmental Impact Assessment, including Heritage Impact Assessments for the Via Troncal Interconectora project as a whole, including the assessment of potential impacts on the landscape areas of Lari Lari, Los Tucos, Cayma and Yanahuara and the identification of mitigation measures, and submit the assessment study to the World Heritage Centre for review by the Advisory Bodies, prior to the approval and implementation of the project; \n 7.&nbsp;   Requests   the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by   1 February 2014   , an updated report on the state of conservation of the property and the implementation of the above for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 38th session in 2014.&nbsp;
