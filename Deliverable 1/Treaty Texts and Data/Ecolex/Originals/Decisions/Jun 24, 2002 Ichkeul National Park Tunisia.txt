The World Heritage Committee:  1.  Notes  that a workshop to define the indicators, benchmarks and other components of a scientific monitoring programme to guide the Committee\'s future assessments of the performance of the rehabilitation programme for the Ichkeul Lake National Park, will be organized in September 2002 using funds provided under an emergency grant of US$50,000 from the World Heritage Fund in May 2002;  2.  Invites  the State Party to submit a detailed report on the results of the emergency assistance project, including a description of the monitoring programme, its financing and plans for its implementation, for examination at its 27th session in June/July 2003; 3.  Decides  to retain the site on the List of World Heritage in Danger.
