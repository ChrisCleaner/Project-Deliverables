The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.    Having examined  Documents  WHC-09/33.COM/8B  and  WHC-09/33.COM/INF.8B1.Add,   \n 2.  Refers  the examination of the proposed buffer zones for the  Punic Town of Kerkuane and its Necropolis ,  Tunisia , back to the State Party to allow it to: \n a) Clearly delineate an area that promotes the integration between the two components of the inscribed serial property to adequately protect and conserve the property. Current land uses and cadastral plans should be considered for the delimitation of the buffer zone; \n b) Provide information regarding the administrative and regulatory measures for the buffer zone as well the prescribed policies for its management.
