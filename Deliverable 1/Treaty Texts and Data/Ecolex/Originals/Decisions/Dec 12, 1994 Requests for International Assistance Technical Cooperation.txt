The Committee took note that the Bureau, at its eighteenth session held on 9-10 December 1994: \n \n approved a sum of  US$ 19,000  for  Srebarna Nature Reserve (Bulgaria)  for the purchase and installation of equipment for the measurement and monitoring of water-levels and water quality in the Srebarna Wetlands; and \n requested the Tanzanian authorities to reformulate their request for US$ 30,000 for developing a system of trails in the  Kilimanjaro National Park (Tanzania),  taking into consideration more urgent conservation priorities for the management of the Park which have been set by the new Management Plan.
