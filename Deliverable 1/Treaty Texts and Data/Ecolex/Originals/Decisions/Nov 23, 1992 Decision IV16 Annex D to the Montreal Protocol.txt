The  Fourth Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. IV/16:      to take note of the entry into force of Annex D to the Protocol on 27 May 1992;   to note that Singapore intends to remove its objection with respect to the products classified under items 1, 2 ( with regard to domestic refrigerators and freezers), 4, 5 and 6 of Annex D;   to adopt the conclusions of the note regarding the Harmonized System customs code numbers for the products listed in Annex D of the amended Montreal Protocol, as contained in document UNEP/OzL.Pro.4/3.
