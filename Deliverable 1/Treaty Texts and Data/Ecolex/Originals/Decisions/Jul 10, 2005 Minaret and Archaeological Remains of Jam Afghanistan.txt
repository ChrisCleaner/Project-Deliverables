The World Heritage Committee, 1.  Having examined  Document   WHC-05/29.COM/7A   and the   Draft Decision  29 C  OM 7A.20.Rev, ,  2.  Recalling  its Decision  28 COM 15A.21 , adopted at its 28th session (Suzhou, 2004),  3.  Urges  the State Party to halt the on-going construction of the road;  4.  Encourages  the State Party to construct an alternative footbridge and a ford across the Hari River, in order to enable access of the villagers from the Bedam Valley to the Jam Valley, as well as allowing a limited number of vehicles to cross the river, following the recommendations of the UNESCO mission in February 2004;  5.  Urges  the State Party of Afghanistan, with assistance from UNESCO and the international community, to continue the on-going efforts for the structural consolidation of the Minaret;  6.  Requests  the State Party, with assistance from the World Heritage Centre and the Advisory Bodies, to elaborate a site-management plan, taking into account the relevant provisions of the  Operational Guidelines  (2005 );   7.  Further requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1  February 2006,  a progress report on the state of conservation of this property, for examination by the Committee at its 30th session (Vilnius, 2006);  8.  Decides  to retain the  Minaret and Archaeological Remains of Jam (Afghanistan)  on the List of World Heritage in Danger.
