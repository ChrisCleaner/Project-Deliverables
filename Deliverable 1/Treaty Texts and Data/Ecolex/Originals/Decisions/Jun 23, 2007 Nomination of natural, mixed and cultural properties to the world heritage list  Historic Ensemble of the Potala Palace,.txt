The World Heritage Committee,   1.  Having examined  Documents WHC-07/31.COM/8B.Add and WHC-07/31.COM/INF.8B1.Add,  2.  Refers  the minor modification to the boundaries of the  Historic Ensemble of the Potala Palace, Lhasa ,  China , back to the State Party to allow it to supplement the information provided on the proposed buffer zones for the three properties by providing:  a) areas and coordinates of the three buffer zones;  b) details of the protective policies in place;  c) rationale for the suggested buffer zones and whether consideration was given to enlarging the area protected.
