The World Heritage Committee,\n1. Thanks the Solomon Islands National Commission for UNESCO for providing\nup to date information and conducting a site visit to assess the state of\nconservation of East Rennell;\n2. Requests IUCN and the World Heritage Centre to organize a joint UNESCO -\nIUCN mission to the property, utilizing expertise from the region to ensure that it\nis cost-effective;\n3. Also requests IUCN and the World Heritage Centre during the mission to:\na) assess and report on the state of conservation of East Rennell,\nb) determine the state of preparation and appropriateness of the Resource\nManagement Plan for the property and the draft national World Heritage\nProtection Bill,\nc) document and assess the effectiveness of the customary protection of the\nproperty.\n4. Further requests IUCN and the World Heritage Centre to present a report on the\noutcome of the mission for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its\n29th session in 2005.
