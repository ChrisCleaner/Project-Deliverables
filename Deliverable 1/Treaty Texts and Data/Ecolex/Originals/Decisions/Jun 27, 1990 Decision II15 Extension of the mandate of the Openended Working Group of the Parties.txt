The  Second Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. II/15  to continue the work of the Open-ended Working Group of the Parties and to extend its mandate to consider, if necessary and in particular, the following topics:     Further elaboration of any remaining details of the various components of the Financial Mechanism;   Identification of the most appropriate modalities for the transfer of technologies designed for the protection of the ozone layer;   Co-operation with Parties that are developing countries for the implementation of the Protocol; and   Problems arising under the trade provisions of the Protocol, in respect of both trade between Parties and trade with non-Parties including issues related to free-trade zones; and to make recommendations to the Third Meeting of the Parties.
