The World Heritage Committee,  1. Having examined Document   WHC-05/29.COM/7B.Rev  ,  2. Recalling its Decision  28 COM 15B.10 , adopted at its 28th session (Suzhou, 2004),  3. Regrets that the State Party of Indonesia did not respond to the specific issues requested;  4. Requests the State Party to urgently submit to the World Heritage Centre, before  1 February 2006 , a progress report on the achievements made to follow-up on the recommendations of 2004 IUCN mission, for examination by the Committee at its 30th session (Vilnius, 2006).
