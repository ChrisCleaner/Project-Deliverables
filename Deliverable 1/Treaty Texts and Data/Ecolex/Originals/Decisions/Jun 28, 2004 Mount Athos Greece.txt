The World Heritage Committee,\n1. Notes with concern damages at the property by the devastating fire at the\nHilandar Monastery;\n2. Expresses its solidarity with the State Party and the orthodox community of the\nMonastery;\n3. Requests the State Party to inform the World Heritage Centre on the current\nstate of conservation of the property and the measures taken to minimise the\ndamage by 1 February 2005, for consideration by the World Heritage\nCommittee at its 29th session in 2005;\n4. Urges the State Party to develop an overall management plan for the World\nHeritage property, which includes the natural values.
