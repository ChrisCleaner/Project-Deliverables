The World Heritage Committee,   1.  Recalling  the decision taken at the 26th session of the Committee in 2002 ( 26 COM 21 (b) 46 ), to carry out a mission to the property and for a report to be provided by the State Party,  2.  Reminds  the State Party of its responsibilities as described in Article 6 of the  World Heritage Convention  to ensure the preservation and conservation of World Heritage properties;  3.  Urgently requests  the World Heritage Centre, the Advisory Bodies and the State Party to work closely together to ensure timely organisation of a joint mission and for a detailed report to be completed in order that the World Heritage Committee can examine the state of conservation of the property at its 28th session in 2004.
