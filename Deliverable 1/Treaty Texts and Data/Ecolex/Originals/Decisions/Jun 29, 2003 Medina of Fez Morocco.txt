The World Heritage Committee,  1.  Takes note  of the report presented by the Secretariat concerning the construction of a concrete slab, covering the Oued Boukhrareb, within the Medina of Fez, and negatively impacting on the World Heritage property;  2.  Requests  the State Party to re-establish the integrity of the property by demolishing this new structure, constructed against the advice of the competent authority;  3.  Invites  the State Party to submit a report to the World Heritage Centre by 1 February 2004 on progress on this matter in order that the World Heritage Committee can examine the state of conservation of the property at its 28th session in 2004.
