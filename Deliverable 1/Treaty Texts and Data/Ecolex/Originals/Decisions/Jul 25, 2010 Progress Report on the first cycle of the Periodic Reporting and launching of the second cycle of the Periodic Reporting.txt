The World Heritage Committee,&nbsp; \n 1.  Having examined  Document WHC-10/34.COM/10C, \n 2.  Recalling  Decisions  31 COM 11D.1 ,  32 COM 11E , and  33 COM 11B , adopted respectively at its   31st session (Christchurch, 2007), 32nd session (Quebec City, 2008), and 33rd session (Seville, 2009), \n 3.  Takes note  of the progress achieved in the follow up to the first cycle of Periodic Reporting, as well as the results of activities in preparation for the launching of its second cycle, and in particular of the sub-regional Workshops organised in Maupiti (French Polynesia, France), Taiyuan (China), and Dehradun (India); \n 4.  Decides  to launch a second cycle of Periodic Reporting in Asia and the Pacific region and  requests  the States Parties of Asia and the Pacific region to actively participate in this process; \n 5.  Also requests  States Parties of Asia and the Pacific region to prepare all the missing retrospective Statements of Outstanding Universal Value concerning properties located in their territory and to submit them to the World Heritage Centre before  1 February 2011  for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 36th session in 2012; \n 6.  Further requests  the World Heritage Centre and the Advisory Bodies to provide assistance to the States Parties in Asia and the Pacific region in the preparation of Periodic Reporting; \n 7.  Requests furthermore  the World Heritage Centre to keep it informed of progress achieved in the Periodic Reporting exercise at its 35th session in 2011.
