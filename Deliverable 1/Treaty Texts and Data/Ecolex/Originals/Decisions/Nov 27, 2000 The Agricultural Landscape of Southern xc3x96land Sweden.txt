Property:  The Agricultural Landscape of Southern &Ouml;land  \n Id. N&deg;:  968  \n State Party:  Sweden    \n Criteria:  C (iv) (v)  \n The Committee decided to inscribe this property on the World Heritage List on the basis of  criteria (iv) and (v):  \n  Criterion (iv) : The landscape of Southern &Ouml;land takes its contemporary form from its long cultural history, adapting to the physical constraints of the geology and topograpy. \n  Criterion (v) : Southern &Ouml;land is an outstanding example of human settlement, making the optimum use of diverse landscape types on a single island. \n Several delegates, as well as IUCN, emphasized that the site was an outstanding example of a continuing landscape which supports and maintains biological diversity. \n The Committee noted the change of name of the property from  S&ouml;dra &Ouml;lands Odlingslandskap (The Agricultural Landscape of Southern &Ouml;land)  to  The Agricultural Landscape of Southern &Ouml;land.  \n &nbsp;
