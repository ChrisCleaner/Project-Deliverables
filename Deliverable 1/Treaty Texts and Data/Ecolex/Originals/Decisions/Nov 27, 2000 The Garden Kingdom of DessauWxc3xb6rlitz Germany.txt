Property:  The Garden Kingdom of Dessau-W&ouml;rlitz  \n Id. N&deg;:  534 Rev  \n State Party:  Germany    \n Criteria:  C (ii) (iv)  \n The Committee decided to inscribe this property on the World Heritage List on the basis of  criteria (ii) and (iv) : \n  Criterion (ii) :   The Garden Kingdom of Dessau-W&ouml;rlitz is an outstanding example of the application of the philosophical principles of the Age of the Enlightenment to the design of a landscape that integrates art, education, and economy in a harmonious whole.   \n  Criterion (iv) : The 18th century was a seminal period for landscape design, of which the Garden Kingdom of Dessau-W&ouml;rlitz is an exceptional and wide-ranging illustration. \n The Committee noted the change of name of the property from  Gartenreich Dessau-W&ouml;rlitz (The Garden Kingdom of Dessau-W&ouml;rlitz, cultural landscape of Dessau-W&ouml;rlitz)  to The Garden Kingdom of Dessau-W&ouml;rlitz. \n &nbsp;
