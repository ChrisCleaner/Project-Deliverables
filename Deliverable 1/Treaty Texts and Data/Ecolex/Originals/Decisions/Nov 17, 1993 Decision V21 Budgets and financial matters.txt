The  Fifth Meeting of the Parties  decided in  Dec. V/21:      To note the financial reports on the Trust Fund for the Montreal Protocol and the Secretariat for the Vienna Convention and the Montreal Protocol for 1992;   To urge all Parties to pay their outstanding contributions promptly and also to pay their future contributions promptly and in full, in accordance with the formula for contributions as set out in Annex III to the report of the Fifth Meeting of the Parties;   To adopt the proposed budgets for the Trust Fund for the Montreal Protocol of US$ 2,822,735 for 1994 and of US$ 3,416,550 for 1995, as set out in Annex IV to the report of the Fifth Meeting of the Parties;   To urge the Secretariat to furnish the Parties with an estimation of the current year\xe2\x80\x99s needs and in the same format the actual expenditures of the previous year so that the Parties will have a good understanding of the Secretariat\xe2\x80\x99s budgetary requirements.
