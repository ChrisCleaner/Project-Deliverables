The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Having examined  Document WHC-12/36.COM/7B.Add, \n 2.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Recalling  Decision  33 COM 7B.77  adopted at its 33rd session (Seville, 2009), \n 3.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Acknowledges  the submission of the revised urban plan which proposes a buffer zone, as well as priority zones for development; \n 4.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Notes  the suspension of the hotel projects near the Mekong River and  requests  the State Party to revise these projects in line with the new urban plan and based on full Heritage Impact Assessments; \n 5.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Regrets  that the airport runway realignment and terminal extension was implemented despite the concerns expressed by the World Heritage Committee and  urges  the State Party not to take any decisions on future developments, which may impact on the Outstanding Universal Value of the property without prior Heritage Impact Assessments and provision of information in line with Paragraph 172 of the  Operational Guidelines ; \n 6.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Requests  the State Party to clarify the current status of proposals for the development of the new town in Chompeth Valley and  also notes  that no details are included in the new urban plan; \n 7.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Also requests  the State Party to submit the Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) and Heritage Impact Assessment (HIA) for the Mekong dam to be constructed 60 km upstream of the property; \n 8.&nbsp;&nbsp;  Further requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 February 2014 , an updated report on the state of conservation of the property and the implementation of the above, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 38th session in 2014.
