The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Noting  that no funds are available in 2004 for consideration of technical co-operation requests for cultural properties, \n 2.  Also noting  that the Bureau did not approve technical co-operation requests in advance on the 2005 budget (Decision 28 BUR 8.3), \n 3.  Agreeing  with the Bureau\'s recommendation not to approve requests in advance on the 2005 budget as it did at its last session in 2003 with regard to the use of the 2004 budget, \n 4.  Decides  that the following request:  Bangladesh : Study of the problems and monitoring of the internal moisture conditions of the monuments of the Buddhist Vihara at Paharpur, be submitted again in 2005 for decision by the Committee, in accordance with the Operational Guidelines in force at that time, provided that the State Party concerned has paid its contributions to the World Heritage Fund as at 31 December 2004.
