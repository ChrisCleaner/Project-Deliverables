The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document  WHC-09/33.COM/7B.Add , \n 2.  Recalling  Decisions  31 COM 8B.24  and  32 COM 8B.102 , adopted at its 31st session (Christchurch, 2007) and 32nd Session (Quebec City, 2008) respectively, \n 3.
