The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Noting  that the issue of observers\' participation in the Committee\'s meetings requires further discussion, as suggested by the State Party of Canada; \n 2.  Requests  the World Heritage Centre to propose amendments to Rule 8 of the  Rules of Procedure  and to circulate these proposals to all States Parties for comment, prior to  1 December 2009 ; \n 3.  Decides  to inscribe the item "Amendment to the Rule 8 of the Rules of Procedure of the World Heritage Committee" on the agenda of the 34th session in 2010.
