The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.&nbsp;&nbsp;   Having examined   Document WHC-13/37.COM/7A, \n 2.&nbsp;&nbsp;   Recalling   Decision   36 COM 7A.24 , adopted at its 36th&nbsp;session (Saint-Petersburg, 2012), \n 3.&nbsp;&nbsp;   Acknowledges   the efforts made by the State Party in the implementation of the corrective measures and   urges   it to secure adequate resources and support to ensure their sustained and comprehensive implementation; \n 4.&nbsp;&nbsp;   Welcomes   the development of the Conservation Plan and   also urges   the State Party to allocate the necessary resources for its implementation; \n 5.&nbsp;&nbsp;   Requests   the State Party to finalize the approval process for regulatory measures for the property, in particular the adoption of the Law of Protection of historic sites, monuments, cities and their urban and cultural heritage, as well as new construction codes, and to ensure their appropriate enforcement; \n 6.&nbsp;&nbsp;   Also requests   the State Party to submit a boundary clarification indicating precisely the boundaries of the property at the time of inscription no later than   1&nbsp;December&nbsp;2013   and a boundary modification request for a buffer zone, according to Annex 11 of the   Operational Guidelines   , no later than   1&nbsp;February&nbsp;2014   ; \n 7.&nbsp;&nbsp;   Further requests   the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by   1&nbsp;February&nbsp;2014   , an updated report on the state of conservation of the property and the implementation of the above, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 38th session in 2014; \n 8.&nbsp;&nbsp;     Decides   to retain the Historic Town of Zabid (Yemen) on the List of World Heritage in Danger.
