The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Documents WHC-11/35.COM/8B.Add and WHC-11/35.COM/INF.8B1.Add, \n 2.  Approves  the proposed minor modification to the boundary of the  Historic Centre of Naples, Italy ; \n 3.  Approves  the proposed buffer zone for the  Historic Centre of Naples, Italy ; \n 4.  Expresses concern  for the recurrent danger, despite the considerable efforts by the State Party, of giving priority to prestigious built structures and areas to the detriment of modest and fragile urban fabric, the intangible heritage, and traditional economic activities and  recommends  that the State Party should allocate part of the funding collected to restoring the balance.
