The World Heritage Committee,   1.  Having examined  Documents WHC-07/31.COM/8B and WHC-07/31.COM/INF.8B.1,  2.  Defers  the examination of the nomination of  Sarazm ,  Tajikistan , to the World Heritage List to allow the State Party to consider submitting a new nomination, in order to:  \xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 a) Explore further the values and significance of the property;  \xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 b) Give consideration to extending the installation of protective covers to all the excavated features on the site;  \xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 c) Reduce the level of excavation on the site and to divert the emphasis to the use of non-invasive techniques of geophysical prospecting for further exploration of the property;  \xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 d) Give consideration to setting up a conservation unit on the site.
