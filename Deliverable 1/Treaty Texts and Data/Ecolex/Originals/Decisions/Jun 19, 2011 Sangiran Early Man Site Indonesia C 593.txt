The World Heritage Committee, \n 1.  Having examined  Document WHC-11/35.COM/7B, \n 2.  Recalling  Decision  33 COM 7B.72 , adopted at its 33rd session (Seville, 2009), \n 3.  Notes  the progress made by the State Party in developing the management system for the site and  acknowledges  the information provided on the actions being taken to implement effective management of the site; \n 4.  Urges  the State Party to continue its work to address the actions recommended at its 33rd session (Seville, 2009), including: \n a) implementing an effective heritage impact assessment process within the property, \n b) involving the residents as key stakeholders of the property; \n 5.  Also urges  the State Party to: \n a) finalise the legal and administrative measures required to protect and manage the property, \n b) develop mechanisms to prevent illegal sand mining at the property; \n 6.  Requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 February 2014 , a progress report on the implementation of the above, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 38th session in 2014.
