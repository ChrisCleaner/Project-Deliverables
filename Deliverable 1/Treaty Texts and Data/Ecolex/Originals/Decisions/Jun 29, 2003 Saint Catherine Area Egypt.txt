The World Heritage Committee,  1.  Decides  not to inscribe the  Saint Catherine Area ,  Egypt , on the World Heritage List on the basis of natural criteria;  2.  Encourges  the State Party to resubmit the nomination as a cultural landscape; and  3.  Notes  that the original inscription of the Saint Catherine Area on the World Heritage List under cultural criteria (i), (iii), (iv) and (vi) is not affected by this decision.
