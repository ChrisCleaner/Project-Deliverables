The World Heritage Committee, \n \n  Having examined  Document WHC-15/39.COM/7B, \n  Recalling  Decision  37 COM 7B.51  adopted at its 37th session (Phnom Penh, 2013), \n  Commends  the State Party on the progress made to manage and conserve the property; \n  Takes note  of the progress in the technical investigation of conservation measures at the Stylite Tower; and  invites  the State Party to undertake dialogue with the World Heritage Centre and the Advisory Bodies before detailed work is planned; \n  Urges  the State Party to complete the management plan which must include a comprehensive conservation plan and archaeological research policy, and integrate a public use plan; \n  Requests  the State Party to submit to the World Heritage Centre, by  1 December 2016 , an updated report, including a 1-page executive summary, on the state of conservation of the property and the implementation of the above, for examination by the World Heritage Committee at its 41st session in 2017.
