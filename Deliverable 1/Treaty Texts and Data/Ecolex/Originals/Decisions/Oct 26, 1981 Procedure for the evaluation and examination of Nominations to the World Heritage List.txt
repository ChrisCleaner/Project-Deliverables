With particular reference to the evaluation and protection of cultural properties, the Committee requested that : \n (a) ICOMOS in the future make comparative evaluations of properties belonging to the same cultural phase or area ; \n (b) ICOMOS prepare for the next Bureau Meeting guidelines for evaluating contemporary architectural structures ; \n (c) the Secretariat examine with ICCROM and ICOMOS the question of protection and management of listed properties and report back to the Committee.
