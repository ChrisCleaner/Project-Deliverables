Galapagos Marine Reserve (Extension of the Galapagos Islands)  \n  1bis  \n  Ecuador    \n The Committee recalled that it deferred the inscription of the Galapagos Marine Reserve at its eighteenth session due to serious threats to the site and in accordance with the IUCN recommendation and the wish of the Observer of Ecuador. \n The Delegate from Ecuador requested that the marine extension of the site be deferred until the twentieth session of the committee. This was agreed by the Committee. \n &nbsp;
