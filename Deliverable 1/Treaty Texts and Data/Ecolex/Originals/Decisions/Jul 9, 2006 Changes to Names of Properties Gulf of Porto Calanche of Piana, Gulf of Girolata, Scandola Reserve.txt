The World Heritage Committee,   1.  Having examined  Document  WHC-06/30.COM/8B ,   2.  Approves  the proposed name change to Cape Girolata, Cape Porto, Scandola Nature Reserve and the Piana Calanches in Corsica as proposed by the French authorities. The name of the property becomes  Gulf of Porto:   Calanche of Piana, Gulf of Girolata, Scandola Reserve  in English   and  Golfe de Porto : calanche de Piana, golfe de Girolata, r\xc3\xa9serve de Scandola  in French .
