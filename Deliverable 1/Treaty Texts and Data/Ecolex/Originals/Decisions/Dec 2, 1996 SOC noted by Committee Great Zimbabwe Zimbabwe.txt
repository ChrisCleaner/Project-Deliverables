VII.72 Great Zimbabwe (Zimbabwe)  \n Considerable progress was reported in the preservation programme for this site managed by National Museums and Monuments of Zimbabwe (NMMZ). A site management plan is in place. As a result of a special Donors Conference held in 1992, the site has also secured surveying equipment. A total survey has been undertaken. \n The Bureau commended the Zimbabwe authorities for their efforts of conservation and the professional expertise which is available  in situ.  It recommended that the World Heritage Committee be kept informed of on-going activities. \n &nbsp;
